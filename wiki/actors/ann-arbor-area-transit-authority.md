The [[actors/ann-arbor-area-transit-authority|Ann Arbor Area Transit Authority]] (AAATA) serves as the party responsible for implementing both the [[initiatives/expand-improve-local-transit|Expand and Improve Local Transit]] and [[initiatives/expand-improve-regional-transit|Expand and Improve Regional Transit]] initiatives under A2Zero Strategy 4 ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). AAATA is tasked with quadrupling current bus ridership, building bus rapid transit along major corridors, and adding 2 new electric buses per year for 10 years ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The authority is also expected to work with the Regional Transit Authority of Southeast Michigan to establish regional transit connections to Detroit and surrounding municipalities by 2021 ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
