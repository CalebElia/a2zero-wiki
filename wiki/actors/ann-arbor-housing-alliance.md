The [[actors/ann-arbor-housing-alliance|Ann Arbor Housing Alliance]] is listed as a collaborator and project co-designer for the [[initiatives/increase-diversity-of-housing|Increase Diversity of Housing]] initiative under A2Zero Strategy 4 ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The alliance contributes to efforts to add 2,000 new units of residential multi-family housing or ADUs in Ann Arbor neighborhoods by 2030, with a focus on affordability for renters and lower-income residents ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
