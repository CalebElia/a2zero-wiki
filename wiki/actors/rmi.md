[[actors/rmi|RMI]] (Rocky Mountain Institute) convened a national cohort of cities and front-line organizations working collaboratively on electrification campaigns, which Ann Arbor joined in Year Two of A2Zero ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). The organization is also listed as a project co-designer for the [[initiatives/green-rental-housing-program|Green Rental Housing Program]] in Ann Arbor's A2Zero plan ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]), contributing expertise in energy efficiency policy for rental housing alongside partners such as the [[actors/washtenaw-area-apartment-association|Washtenaw Area Apartment Association]] and [[actors/michigan-saves|Michigan Saves]] ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
