The Washtenaw Area Apartment Association is a project co-designer for the [[initiatives/green-rental-housing-program|Green Rental Housing Program]], representing rental property owners in the policy development process ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The program integrates energy efficiency requirements into the City's rental licensing process, making stakeholder engagement with landlord associations a key component of program design ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
