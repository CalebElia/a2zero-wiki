The Engineering Department within the [[actors/city-of-ann-arbor|City of Ann Arbor]] is responsible for public infrastructure design and delivery. It houses the [[actors/ann-arbor-engineering-department-transportation-team|Transportation Team]], which leads active mobility, traffic planning, and EV-charging-infrastructure work as part of A2Zero implementation.
