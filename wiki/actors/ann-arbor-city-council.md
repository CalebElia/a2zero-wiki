The Ann Arbor City Council is the elected legislative body of the City of Ann Arbor ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). In November 2019, the Council passed Resolution 19-2103, directing the City to develop a living plan for community-wide carbon neutrality by 2030 and to present it by Earth Day 2020, thereby authorizing the [[initiatives/a2zero-carbon-neutrality-plan|A2Zero]] initiative ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).

The resolution was sponsored by [[actors/christopher-taylor|Mayor Christopher Taylor]] and Councilmembers [[actors/jeff-bannister|Jeff Bannister]], [[actors/zachary-smith|Zachary Smith]], [[actors/ali-ramlawi|Ali Ramlawi]], [[actors/julie-grand|Julie Grand]], [[actors/chip-ackerman|Chip Ackerman]], [[actors/jack-eaton|Jack Eaton]], [[actors/chip-nelson|Chip Nelson]], and [[actors/anne-marie-griswold|Anne Marie Griswold]] ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
