IBEW Local 252 is the International Brotherhood of Electrical Workers local union that partnered with Ann Arbor's Office of Sustainability and Innovations during Year One of A2ZERO to develop a home electrification assessment ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]). This collaboration connects organized labor's technical expertise with the city's building electrification goals ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]).

IBEW Local 252 works alongside NECA (the National Electrical Contractors Association), Ann Arbor's local electrical contractor association, continuing their collaboration with [[actors/office-of-sustainability-and-innovations|OSI]] into Year 3 of A2ZERO on electrification readiness ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). IBEW/NECA launched a campaign called 'The Future is Electric,' releasing a series of informational videos to help residents understand what is involved in upgrading their homes for electrification of appliances and vehicles ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). Workforce development conversations were also initiated as part of this collaboration ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]).
