Ann Arbor Building, Rental, and Inspection Services is the City department responsible for leading implementation of the [[initiatives/transition-to-more-energy-efficient-homes-and-businesses|Transition to More Energy Efficient Homes and Businesses]] and [[initiatives/update-building-codes|Update Building Codes]] initiatives under the A2Zero plan ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The department works in partnership with the [[actors/office-of-sustainability-and-innovations|Office of Sustainability and Innovations]] on both initiatives and is responsible for training inspectors and enforcing updated building codes once adopted ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
