The [[actors/ann-arbor-historic-district-commission|Ann Arbor Historic District Commission]] is identified as a collaborator on both the [[initiatives/increase-diversity-of-housing|Increase Diversity of Housing]] and [[initiatives/develop-mixed-use-neighborhoods|Develop Mixed-Use Neighborhoods]] initiatives under A2Zero Strategy 4 ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The commission's involvement reflects the need to balance new housing and mixed-use zoning changes with the preservation of Ann Arbor's neighborhood character and historic integrity ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
