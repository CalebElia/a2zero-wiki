[[actors/elevate-energy|Elevate Energy]] is a nonprofit organization that partnered with the [[actors/office-of-sustainability-and-innovations|Office of Sustainability and Innovations]] during the A2Zero planning process ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Elevate Energy was engaged to assess the greenhouse gas reduction potential and costs associated with each action included in the final A2Zero plan ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
