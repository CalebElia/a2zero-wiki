El Harissa Market Café is one of the three founding participating businesses in the [[initiatives/a2r3-reduce-reuse-return|A2R3 Reduce, Reuse, Return]] program, offering reusable containers for takeout orders ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). Customers can return containers to any participating business to be cleaned and reused ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
