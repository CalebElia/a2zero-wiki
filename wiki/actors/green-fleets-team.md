The [[actors/green-fleets-team|Green Fleets Team]] (GFT) was established following City Council Resolution R-17-237 in June 2017 to update the City's Green Fleets Policy and develop a plan for electrifying the City fleet ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The GFT was given until the first quarter of 2020 to produce a fleet electrification plan targeting 90% electrification by 2025, focusing first on light-duty vehicles before addressing medium- and heavy-duty equipment ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
