[[actors/ibew-neca|IBEW/NECA]] is the local Electrical Workers Union that partnered with the City of Ann Arbor in Year Two of A2Zero to launch the joint "The Future is Electric" campaign ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). The partnership aims to bolster electrification readiness among Ann Arbor residents ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
