The [[actors/washtenaw-area-transportation-study|Washtenaw Area Transportation Study]] (WATS) is listed as a collaborator on the [[initiatives/expand-improve-local-transit|Expand and Improve Local Transit]], [[initiatives/expand-improve-regional-transit|Expand and Improve Regional Transit]], and [[initiatives/park-and-ride-expansion|Park and Ride Expansion]] initiatives under A2Zero Strategy 4 ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). WATS provides transportation planning coordination across Washtenaw County to support the VMT reduction goals of the A2Zero plan ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
