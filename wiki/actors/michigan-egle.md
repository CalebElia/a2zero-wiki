The Michigan Department of Environment, Great Lakes, and Energy (EGLE) is a state agency that provided grant funding to Ann Arbor's A2Zero initiatives ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). In Year Two of A2Zero (2021–2022), EGLE awarded $8,500 for the [[initiatives/contractor-electrification-education-badging|Contractor Education and Badging Program on Electrification]] ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
