The Rocky Mountain Institute is listed as a project co-designer for the [[initiatives/green-rental-housing-program|Green Rental Housing Program]] in Ann Arbor's A2Zero plan ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The organization contributes expertise in energy efficiency policy for rental housing alongside partners such as the [[actors/washtenaw-area-apartment-association|Washtenaw Area Apartment Association]] and [[actors/michigan-saves|Michigan Saves]] ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
