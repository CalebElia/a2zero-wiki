The Transportation Team is a unit within the [[actors/city-engineering-department|City Engineering Department]] of the [[actors/city-of-ann-arbor|City of Ann Arbor]]. It focuses on public infrastructure, traffic planning, and active mobility — including non-motorized transportation planning, park-and-ride expansion, and tiered parking rate policy. Coordinates with the [[actors/ann-arbor-transportation-commission|Transportation Commission]] on transit and mobility work.
