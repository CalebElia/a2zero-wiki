[[actors/theride|TheRide]] (Ann Arbor Area Transportation Authority) is the regional public transit provider serving Ann Arbor and surrounding communities ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The A2Zero plan identifies TheRide as a key partner for bus fleet electrification, route expansion, and rapid transit development along major corridors ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The plan also proposes expanding the Go-Pass program area and improving bus stop infrastructure with solar-powered shelters as part of TheRide's role in the city's carbon neutrality strategy ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
