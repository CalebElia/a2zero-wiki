The Ann Arbor Downtown Development Authority (DDA) is a government body that supports economic development in Ann Arbor's downtown district ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). The DDA is a project co-designer for the [[initiatives/green-business-challenge|Green Business Challenge]], which initially targets downtown businesses before expanding to any local business in Ann Arbor ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The DDA's involvement reflects the program's initial geographic focus on the downtown business district ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The DDA was also identified as a partner in the deployment of 50 additional EV chargers as part of A2Zero's Year 3 priorities ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
