Bright Cities is an organization that provided $5,000 in funding to support tree planting in underserved areas of Ann Arbor ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). This grant was secured during Year Two of A2Zero (2021–2022) as part of the [[initiatives/tree-planting-underserved-areas|Tree Planting in Underserved Areas]] initiative ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
