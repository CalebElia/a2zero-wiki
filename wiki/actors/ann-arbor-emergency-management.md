[[actors/ann-arbor-emergency-management|Ann Arbor Emergency Management]] is a City of Ann Arbor government office responsible for emergency preparedness and hazard mitigation ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). In Year Two of A2Zero, the office worked with the A2Zero team to integrate climate change and resilience projects into the City's updated multi-hazard mitigation plan, and supported the staffing of an emergency preparedness shelter during a 5-day power outage in August 2021 ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
