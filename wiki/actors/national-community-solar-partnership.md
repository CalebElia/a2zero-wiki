The National Community Solar Partnership (NCSP) is a federal program that awarded Ann Arbor a technical assistance grant to support development of a low-to-moderate income solar access program during Year One of A2ZERO ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]). The grant provides expertise and resources to help the city design an equitable community solar program ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]).
