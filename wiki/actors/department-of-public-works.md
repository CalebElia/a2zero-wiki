The [[actors/department-of-public-works|Department of Public Works]] (DPW) is the primary implementing agency for all six actions under [[strategies/strategy-5-materials-waste|A2Zero Strategy 5]], including composting expansion, commercial recycling, construction waste policy, circular economy, and refrigerant recycling ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). DPW leads implementation in partnership with the [[actors/office-of-sustainability-and-innovations|Office of Sustainability and Innovations]] across most Strategy 5 initiatives, and works with external partners such as [[actors/resource-recycling-associates|Resource Recycling Associates]] on commercial recycling and sustainable materials programs ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
