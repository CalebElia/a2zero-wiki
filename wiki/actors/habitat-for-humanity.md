Habitat for Humanity is listed as a project co-designer for the [[initiatives/expand-weatherization-program|Expand Weatherization Program]] in Ann Arbor, contributing to efforts to extend weatherization services to more low-income homeowners than the County program alone can serve ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The organization works alongside the [[actors/washtenaw-county-office-of-community-and-economic-development|Washtenaw County Office of Community and Economic Development]], [[actors/michigan-saves|Michigan Saves]], and utilities in program delivery ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
