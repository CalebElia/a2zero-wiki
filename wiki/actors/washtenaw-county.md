[[actors/washtenaw-county|Washtenaw County]] was one of 67 organizations that signed on as an A2Zero partner by early March 2020, agreeing to co-host events, share information with its networks, and display the A2Zero partner logo ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Washtenaw County coordinated with [[actors/office-of-sustainability-and-innovations|OSI]] and Ann Arbor Public Schools to complete greenhouse gas emissions inventories in Year 3 ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). The County also provided a $4,000 Waste Reduction Sponsorship to advance the A2R3 returnable containers program ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). The City applied for an EPA grant to expand resilience hubs throughout Washtenaw County as part of a broader county-wide resilience network ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]).
