The Ann Arbor Fire Department is a City of Ann Arbor department that partnered with the City's sustainability team on the design of what is described as the State's first carbon neutral fire station ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). As of Year Two, the City was actively working with the fire department to find funding to operationalize the carbon neutral design ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
