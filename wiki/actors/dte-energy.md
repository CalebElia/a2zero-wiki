[[actors/dte-energy|DTE Energy]] serves as the existing utility provider for Ann Arbor and is a named collaborator on both the Community Solar Program and the Landfill Solar Project under A2Zero Strategy 1 ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Under the CCA model, DTE would continue to provide transmission and distribution services even as the City procures power from an alternative supplier ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). For the Landfill Solar Project, DTE is a co-developer alongside the City and Pittsfield Township, with grid upgrade costs at the point of intersection still to be determined ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
