The [[actors/regional-transit-authority-of-southeast-michigan|Regional Transit Authority of Southeast Michigan]] (RTA) is identified as a supporting partner to the [[actors/ann-arbor-area-transit-authority|Ann Arbor Area Transit Authority]] for both local and regional transit expansion under A2Zero Strategy 4 ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The RTA's role encompasses supporting the development of high-capacity transit and express transit systems connecting Ann Arbor with nearby jurisdictions including Detroit, Ypsilanti, Chelsea, and Brighton ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
