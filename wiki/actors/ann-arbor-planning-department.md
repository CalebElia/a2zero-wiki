The [[actors/ann-arbor-planning-department|Ann Arbor Planning Department]] is the party responsible for implementing both the [[initiatives/increase-diversity-of-housing|Increase Diversity of Housing]] and [[initiatives/develop-mixed-use-neighborhoods|Develop Mixed-Use Neighborhoods]] initiatives under A2Zero Strategy 4, with support from the Planning Commission and City Council ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The department is tasked with completing a Comprehensive Master Plan Update by 2021 and adopting corresponding zoning code amendments by 2024 to enable mixed-use development and increased housing density ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Planning staff are also expected to review the zoning code in 2028 and make suggestions to Council for further changes ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
