Elevate is a nonprofit organization that partnered with the [[actors/office-of-sustainability-and-innovations|Office of Sustainability and Innovations]] during the A2Zero planning process, where it was engaged to assess the greenhouse gas reduction potential and costs associated with each action included in the final A2Zero plan ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Elevate also partners with the City of Ann Arbor, the [[actors/community-action-network|Community Action Network]], and the [[actors/university-of-michigan|University of Michigan]] on the [[initiatives/bryant-neighborhood-decarbonization|Bryant Neighborhood Decarbonization]] initiative, which aims to design and create the first fully decarbonized existing neighborhood in America in the Bryant neighborhood ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
