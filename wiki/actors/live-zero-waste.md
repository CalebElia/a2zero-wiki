Live Zero Waste is a partner organization involved in the [[initiatives/a2r3-reduce-reuse-return|A2R3 Reduce, Reuse, Return]] program in Ann Arbor ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). The organization maintains up-to-date program information and allows residents to check out reusable containers when visiting participating businesses ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
