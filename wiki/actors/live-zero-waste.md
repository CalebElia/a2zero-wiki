Live Zero Waste is a nonprofit organization and partner involved in the [[initiatives/a2r3-reduce-reuse-return|A2R3 Reduce, Reuse, Return]] program in Ann Arbor ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). The organization partnered with the City of Ann Arbor's [[actors/office-of-sustainability-and-innovations|OSI]] to maintain the A2R3 returnable containers program at four local restaurants in Year 3 ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). Live Zero Waste maintains up-to-date program information and allows residents to check out reusable containers when visiting participating businesses ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]), supporting Ann Arbor's circular economy goals by facilitating reusable container systems in the food service sector ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]).
