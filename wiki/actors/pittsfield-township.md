Pittsfield Township is a partner in the Landfill Solar Project, with the City of Ann Arbor holding land in a PUD arrangement with the township for the solar installation. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]) A portion of the clean energy generated is designated to offset Pittsfield Township's municipal operations. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]])
