[[actors/pittsfield-township|Pittsfield Township]] is a named partner in the Landfill Solar Project, with the solar installation sited partly on land held in a Planned Unit Development (PUD) with the township ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). A portion of the clean energy generated by the project is designated to offset Pittsfield Township's municipal operations ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
