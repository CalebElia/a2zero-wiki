The City of Ann Arbor Signs and Signals department is the party responsible for implementing the [[initiatives/led-street-lighting-and-traffic-signals|LED Street Lighting and Traffic Signals]] initiative, overseeing the conversion of all municipal streetlights and traffic signals to LED technology by 2029 ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The department works with the [[actors/office-of-sustainability-and-innovations|Office of Sustainability and Innovations]] and [[actors/dte-energy|DTE Energy]], including negotiating the purchase of streetlights currently owned by DTE ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
