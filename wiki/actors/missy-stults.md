Missy Stults (mstults@a2gov.org) is a City of Ann Arbor sustainability staff member responsible for activities supporting Strategy 1 of the A2Zero plan, covering renewable energy grid initiatives ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). She was recognized by GRIST as one of the 50 "Fixers" of 2022 during Year Two of A2Zero ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
