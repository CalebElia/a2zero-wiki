ICLEI-Local Governments for Sustainability is an international nonprofit organization working with local governments on sustainability initiatives ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). In Ann Arbor's A2Zero program, ICLEI is partnering with the City to establish a series of circular economy metrics as part of the [[initiatives/circular-economy-working-group|Circular Economy Working Group]] ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
