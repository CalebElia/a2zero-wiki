The [[actors/university-of-michigan|University of Michigan]] was among the 67 organizations that signed on as A2Zero partner organizations by early March 2020 ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The University of Michigan's President's Commission on Carbon Neutrality was also separately listed as a partner, reflecting the institution's multi-faceted engagement with the A2Zero planning process ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
