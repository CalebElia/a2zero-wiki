The [[actors/university-of-michigan|University of Michigan]] is a major research university located in Ann Arbor and a key institutional partner in A2Zero climate initiatives ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). The university was among the 67 organizations that signed on as A2Zero partner organizations by early March 2020 ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The University of Michigan's President's Commission on Carbon Neutrality was also separately listed as a partner, reflecting the institution's multi-faceted engagement with the A2Zero planning process ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The university was additionally identified as a collaborator in the deployment of 50 additional EV chargers as part of A2Zero's Year 3 priorities ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
