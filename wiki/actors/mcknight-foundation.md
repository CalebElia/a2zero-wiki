The McKnight Foundation is a philanthropic organization that awarded $75,000 to support the [[initiatives/bryant-neighborhood-decarbonization|Bryant Neighborhood Decarbonization]] initiative in Ann Arbor ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). This funding was secured during Year Two of A2Zero (2021–2022) ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
