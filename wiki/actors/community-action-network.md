The Community Action Network is a nonprofit partner that collaborated with the City of Ann Arbor's Office of Sustainability and Innovations on the Bryant Neighborhood Decarbonization Initiative during Year One of A2ZERO ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]). The partnership focused on co-creating a whole home health, safety, comfort, and decarbonization assessment with Bryant neighborhood residents ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]). The Community Action Network is also listed as a collaborator and project co-designer for Ann Arbor's [[initiatives/a2zero-ambassadors-program|Neighborhood and Youth Ambassador Programs]] under A2Zero Strategy 6 ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
