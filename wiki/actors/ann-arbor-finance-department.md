The Ann Arbor Finance Department is a project co-designer for the [[initiatives/loan-loss-reserve|Loan Loss Reserve]] initiative, collaborating with [[actors/michigan-saves|Michigan Saves]] and local and regional banks to structure the $1,000,000 reserve fund that enables lenders to extend loans to low-income residents for energy efficiency and renewable energy improvements ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
