Ann Arbor SPARK is a project co-designer for the [[initiatives/green-business-challenge|Green Business Challenge]], contributing to business recruitment and engagement for the sustainability program ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The organization works alongside the [[actors/ann-arbor-ypsilanti-regional-chamber-of-commerce|Ann Arbor-Ypsilanti Regional Chamber of Commerce]] and other partners to support local businesses in reducing their environmental footprints ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
