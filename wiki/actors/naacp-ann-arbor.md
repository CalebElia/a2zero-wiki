The [[actors/naacp-ann-arbor|NAACP Ann Arbor Chapter]] (National Association for the Advancement of Colored People) was one of 67 organizations that signed on as an A2Zero partner by early March 2020, supporting the initiative's goal of engaging underrepresented constituencies in the carbon neutrality planning process ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
