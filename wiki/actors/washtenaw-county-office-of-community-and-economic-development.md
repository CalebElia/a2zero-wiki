The Washtenaw County Office of Community and Economic Development is the party responsible for implementing the [[initiatives/expand-weatherization-program|Expand Weatherization Program]] under A2Zero, administering funds provided by the City of Ann Arbor through a Council-approved contract ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The department also collaborates on the [[initiatives/aging-in-place-efficiently|Aging in Place Efficiently]] program, which is designed to expand weatherization services to low-income seniors beyond what the County's existing program can serve ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Additionally, the department is listed as a collaborator and project co-designer for Ann Arbor's [[initiatives/resilience-hubs|Resilience Hubs]] initiative under A2Zero Strategy 6 ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
