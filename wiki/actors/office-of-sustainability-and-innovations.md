The Office of Sustainability and Innovations (OSI) was created in FY19 to actualize the sustainability goals created and reaffirmed by Ann Arbor City Council ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). OSI developed a 5-year work plan aimed at achieving a 25% reduction in community-wide greenhouse gas emissions by 2025 relative to 2000 levels, and held funding in its FY2020 budget for the public engagement process that would lead to the A2Zero plan ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
