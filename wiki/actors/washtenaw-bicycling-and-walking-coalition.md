The [[actors/washtenaw-bicycling-and-walking-coalition|Washtenaw Bicycling and Walking Coalition]] is listed as a collaborator and project co-designer for the [[initiatives/non-motorized-transportation-plan|Implement Non-Motorized Transportation Plan]] initiative under A2Zero Strategy 4 ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The coalition contributes to efforts to achieve 25% of in-city trips through non-motorized transit by 2030 through safe and ubiquitous active transportation infrastructure ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
