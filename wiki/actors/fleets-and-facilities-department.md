The [[actors/fleets-and-facilities-department|Fleets and Facilities Department]] is the party responsible for implementing the Electrify City Fleet initiative, with support from the [[actors/office-of-sustainability-and-innovations|Office of Sustainability and Innovations]] ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The department oversees the transition of the City's vehicle fleet to electric, prioritizing light-duty vehicles and integrating updated fleet replacement rates into the City budget starting in 2021 ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
