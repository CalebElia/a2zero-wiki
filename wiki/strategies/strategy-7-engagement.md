Strategy 7 of the A2Zero Carbon Neutrality Plan is listed as 'Other' and encompasses additional cross-cutting actions needed to fully achieve carbon neutrality. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]) The seven strategies together are supported by 44 specific actions determined through public input, technical advisory committees, peer community guidance, and staff expertise. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]])

<!-- source: cap-2020 -->
This strategy covers programs and activities that do not fit into other A2Zero strategy areas, including equity programs, small grants, an internal carbon tax, and greenhouse gas offsets. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]) Combined, the four actions under this strategy account for just under 14% of community-wide emissions reductions and are estimated to cost just under $6,000,000. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]])
