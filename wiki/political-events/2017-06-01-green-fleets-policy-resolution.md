City Council Resolution R-17-237, approved in June 2017, directed the City Administrator to update and revive the City's Green Fleets Policy and promote the purchase of plug-in electric vehicles ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The resolution incorporated updated best practices and lessons learned from peer municipalities, and gave the Green Fleets Team until the first quarter of 2020 to develop a plan for electrifying the City fleet by 2025 ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
