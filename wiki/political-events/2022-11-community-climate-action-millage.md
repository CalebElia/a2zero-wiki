In November 2022, Ann Arbor voters passed the Community Climate Action Millage, a 20-year self-imposed tax to fund local climate action ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). This vote made Ann Arbor the 8th community in America to tax itself to fund local climate action ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]).
