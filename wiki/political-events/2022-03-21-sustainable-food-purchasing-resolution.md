On March 21, 2022, Ann Arbor City Council passed a resolution incorporating recommendations for a sustainable food purchasing policy that applies to all City purchases of food items ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). The recommendations had been submitted on February 1, 2022, as part of the A2Zero [[initiatives/sustainable-food-purchasing-policy|Sustainable Food Purchasing Policy]] initiative ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
