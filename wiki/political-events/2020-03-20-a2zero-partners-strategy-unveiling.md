On March 20, 2020, OSI held the final A²ZERO partner-only meeting, presenting the completed carbon neutrality strategy to the network of 67 partner organizations before the plan's public release on Earth Day 2020 ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). This was the third in a series of partner convenings that began with the first partner meeting on December 13, 2019 and an equity and climate training on December 19, 2019. The unveiling marked the close of the intensive community co-design phase and the transition to public launch ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
