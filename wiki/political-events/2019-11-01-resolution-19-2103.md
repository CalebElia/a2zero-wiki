In November 2019, Ann Arbor City Council passed Resolution 19-2103, calling on the City to work with the public to create a living plan for achieving community-wide carbon neutrality by 2030 ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The resolution required the plan to be presented by Earth Day 2020 ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
