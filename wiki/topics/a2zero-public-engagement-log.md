The A²ZERO planning process included 68 public events between November 13, 2019 and March 12, 2020 — an average of roughly one event every two days across a 120-day window. Events ranged from formal commission presentations to neighborhood coffee hours, tabling at farmers markets, and youth sessions at high schools. The breadth of venues was deliberate: OSI used partner networks to reach renters, seniors, students, lower-income residents, and non-English speakers who typically miss city planning processes. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]])
