The Expand Commercial Recycling initiative uses incentives, education, and enforcement policies to bring commercial sector recycling rates to at least 30%. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]) The Solid Waste Resource Management Plan estimates a range of 1,700 to 4,400 tons of waste diverted from landfill as a result of increased commercial sector recycling participation. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]) The 10-year cost of the program is $18,400,000, with a greenhouse gas reduction potential of 5,600 metric tons of CO₂ equivalent, representing 0.3% of community-wide emissions. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]])
