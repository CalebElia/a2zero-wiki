The A2Zero plan proposed creating a city-level on-bill financing program for renewable energy and energy efficiency projects, distinct from the DTE-specific on-bill financing concept also considered ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). On-bill financing allows customers to repay clean energy investments through their utility bills, eliminating the need for upfront capital ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
