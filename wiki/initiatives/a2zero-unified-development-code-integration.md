The City of Ann Arbor launched a collaboration between [[actors/a2zero|A2Zero]] staff and the [[actors/city-of-ann-arbor-planning-department|Planning Department]] to integrate A2Zero climate goals into the Unified Development Code ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). This effort aims to embed VMT-reduction and sustainability objectives directly into the land-use regulatory framework governing development in Ann Arbor, ensuring that planning and zoning decisions support decarbonization objectives ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). The initiative also extends to Ann Arbor's comprehensive land use plan, and achieving this integration was identified as a Year 3 priority in the A2Zero Year 2 Annual Report ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
