The [[actors/city-of-ann-arbor-planning-department|Planning Department]] removed parking minimums from plan review requirements as part of Ann Arbor's effort to reduce vehicle miles traveled under [[strategies/strategy-4-vmt-reduction|Strategy 4]] ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). Eliminating parking minimums is a land-use reform intended to reduce car dependency and support more walkable, transit-oriented development patterns ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
