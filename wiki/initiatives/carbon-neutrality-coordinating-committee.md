The Carbon Neutrality Coordinating Committee was launched during Year One of A2ZERO to coordinate efforts across stakeholders working toward Ann Arbor's carbon neutrality goals ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]). The committee represents a governance mechanism for aligning city departments, community partners, and other actors on A2ZERO implementation ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]).
