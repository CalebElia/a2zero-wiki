The LED Streetlight Conversion Program is a city-wide initiative to replace all existing City of Ann Arbor streetlights with energy-efficient LED fixtures ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). The program is funded by a $980,000 SEMCOG Carbon Reduction Program grant, which was won during Year 3 of A2ZERO, with work slated to begin in fall 2023 ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). The initiative is identified as a Year Four priority for the [[actors/office-of-sustainability-and-innovations|Office of Sustainability and Innovations]] ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]).
