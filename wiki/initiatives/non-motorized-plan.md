The Non-Motorized Plan is projected to reduce 60,400 MT CO2e, representing 2.8% of total community emissions, at a total cost of $2,400,000 and approximately $40 per ton ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Co-benefits include air quality improvement, resilience, public health, economic savings, and equity benefits ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
