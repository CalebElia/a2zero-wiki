Increasing carbon sequestration practices across Ann Arbor was identified in the A2Zero plan as a strategy to remove carbon dioxide from the atmosphere through natural and land-based approaches ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). This concept encompasses practices such as expanding urban tree canopy, green infrastructure, and other nature-based solutions that store carbon within the city ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
