The A2Zero plan proposes implementing and leveraging sensors and data to monitor heat, air quality, waterways, and floods, with drones also identified as a tool for data collection and disaster response ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Tracking public health metrics from the state and CDC is identified as a complementary data action ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
