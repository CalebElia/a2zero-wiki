The A2ZERO Ambassadors Program trains community members to serve as sustainability educators and project leaders in their neighborhoods. In Year One, 22 community members were trained as A2ZERO Ambassadors, and they designed and implemented seven sustainability-focused community projects ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]). The program also developed educational materials for Ambassadors to use in their neighborhoods ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]). Year Two plans included training a second cohort of Ambassadors ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]).
