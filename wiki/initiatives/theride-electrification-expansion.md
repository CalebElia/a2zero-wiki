The A2Zero plan proposes electrifying [[actors/ann-arbor-area-transit-authority|TheRide]] bus vehicles and expanding bus routes to connect more of the city, including providing rapid transit along major corridors ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Additional ideas include powering bus stop shelters with solar power for security lighting, cameras, and information displays, and using better technology to make the bus system more accurate and on-time ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The plan also calls for creating bus and carpool lanes and "right-sizing" transit vehicles to improve efficiency ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
