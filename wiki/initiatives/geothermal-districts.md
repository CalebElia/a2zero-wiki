Geothermal districts were proposed in the A2Zero plan as a shared infrastructure approach to providing low-carbon heating and cooling across multiple buildings or neighborhoods ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). District geothermal systems can achieve greater efficiency and lower per-unit costs than individual building installations by sharing ground loop infrastructure ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
