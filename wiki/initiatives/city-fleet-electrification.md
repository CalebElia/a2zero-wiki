Ann Arbor's City Fleet Electrification initiative aims to transition municipal vehicles from fossil fuels to electric power. In Year One, the city transitioned 10% of its light duty fleet to electric, including making its first EV purchase for the Police Department ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]). Year Two plans included commissioning the city's first all-electric refuse truck ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]).
