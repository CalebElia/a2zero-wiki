The A2Zero plan proposed creating bulk-buy purchasing opportunities for renewable energy, energy efficiency upgrades, and electric vehicles and infrastructure as a way to reduce costs and accelerate adoption ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Group purchasing programs can lower per-unit costs for residents and businesses, making clean energy transitions more financially accessible ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
