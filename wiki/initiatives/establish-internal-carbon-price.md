The Establish Internal Carbon Price initiative creates a financial mechanism whereby all City Service Areas integrate greenhouse gas emissions into decision-making through an internal carbon tax, with each service area paying per ton of greenhouse gases emitted. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]) Funds collected are returned in the form of energy efficiency improvements and renewable energy installations at City facilities. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]) The 10-year estimated cost is $75,000, and greenhouse gas reduction potential was not calculated. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]])
