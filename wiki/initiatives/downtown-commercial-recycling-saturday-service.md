The City of Ann Arbor set up Saturday service for downtown commercial recycling, with service scheduled to begin July 1, 2022 ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). This initiative expands recycling access for commercial entities in the downtown area as part of [[strategies/strategy-5-materials-waste|Strategy 5]] of the A2Zero plan ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
