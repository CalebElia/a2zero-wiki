title: Transit-Oriented Development and Upzoning
type: initiative

The plan proposes upzoning to encourage density and affordability, with zoning allowances for mixed-use neighborhoods, walkable grocery stores, tiny houses, and accessory dwelling units (ADUs) ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Transit-oriented urban planning along transit corridors and building affordable workforce housing near workplaces to reduce commuting are also proposed ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Reducing parking availability and eliminating parking minimums are identified as complementary policy actions ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
