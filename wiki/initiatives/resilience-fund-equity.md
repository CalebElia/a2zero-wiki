The A2Zero plan proposes creating a resilience fund with a focus on equity as part of residential and commercial support for climate resilience ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Complementary support actions include defining and educating residents on best management practices, educating on DTE Energy support for energy efficiency upgrades, and strengthening building codes for efficiency and weatherization ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Requiring 2030 District standards across all buildings is also identified as a building performance measure ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
