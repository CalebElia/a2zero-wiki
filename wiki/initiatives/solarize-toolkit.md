The Solarize Toolkit is a step-by-step guide developed by Ann Arbor to help other communities implement the Solarize group-purchasing solar model ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]). It was launched during Year One of A2ZERO as a resource for replicating Ann Arbor's residential solar bulk-buy approach ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]).
