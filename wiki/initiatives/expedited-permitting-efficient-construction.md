type: initiative

The A2Zero plan proposed expediting the permitting process for more energy-efficient construction as an administrative incentive to encourage developers and property owners to build to higher performance standards ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Streamlined permitting reduces the time and cost burden of green building projects, making efficient construction more competitive ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
