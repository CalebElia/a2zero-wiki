The A2Zero plan proposed energy disclosure policies covering rental, single-family, and commercial buildings as a transparency mechanism to drive efficiency improvements ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Disclosure requirements create market incentives for building owners to improve energy performance by making energy use data visible to tenants, buyers, and the public ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
