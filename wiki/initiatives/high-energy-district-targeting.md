The A2Zero plan proposed targeting high-energy-use districts — both residential and commercial — with concentrated interventions in renewables, energy efficiency, and electrification ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). This place-based approach would focus resources where energy consumption and emissions reduction potential are greatest ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
