title: Composting Expansion and Local Organics Recovery
type: initiative

The A2Zero plan envisions expanding composting pickup to be accessible to commercial, industrial, and multi-family properties, improving ease of use, and enabling year-round composting ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Local composting initiatives considered include neighborhood-scale composting, returning compost to local farmers, providing free compost to residents, and creating a compost market ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The plan also calls for encouraging and incentivizing homeowner composting and providing information on the environmental impacts of different compostable materials ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Methane recovery on wastewater is identified as a cross-cutting organics and energy opportunity ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
