The A2Zero plan proposes improving composting pickup to make it accessible to commercial, industrial, and multi-family users, easier to use, and available year-round ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Local composting options are also identified, including neighborhood composting, returning compost to local farmers, providing free compost to residents, and creating a compost market ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
