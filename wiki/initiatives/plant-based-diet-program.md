The A2Zero plan identifies scaling pledge programs such as the Blue Zone Program as a strategy to promote plant-based diets as part of resource reduction ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Incorporating Ann Arbor Public Schools and other institutions into plant-based diet initiatives is also considered, along with providing cooking classes focused on plant-based, local, and seasonal foods through Recreation and Education programs ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Education on and potential bans of commodity beef and CAFO meat are listed as additional policy levers ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
