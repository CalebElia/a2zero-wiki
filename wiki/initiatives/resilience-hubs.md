The A2Zero plan identifies development and leveraging of existing infrastructure as resilience hubs as a key preparedness action, including activating some homes as shelters with resources and energy ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The plan also calls for identifying safe gathering points in neighborhoods during power outages and extreme heat or cold events ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Needs mapping of neighborhoods based on specific resident support requirements is proposed to inform hub placement and services ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
