The Electrification Contractor Training and Badging Program was launched in Year Two of A2Zero to ensure contractors are knowledgeable about electrification opportunities in Michigan ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). The program connects residents with qualified professionals to assist in the transition to home electrification, with contractors listed on the [[actors/michigan-saves|Michigan Saves]] Locator ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
