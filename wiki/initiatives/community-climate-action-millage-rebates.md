A series of residential rebates and incentives programs is planned for Year Four, made possible by the recently passed Community Climate Action Millage ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). These programs are expected to span Strategies 1, 2, and 3, accelerating rooftop solar, building electrification, and energy efficiency improvements for Ann Arbor residents ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]).
