A resilience hub was created at Northside Community Center during Year One of A2ZERO, featuring a solar-powered battery storage system that provides up to three days of power in case of an outage ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]). Grant funding was also secured for the creation of an additional resilience hub ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]).
