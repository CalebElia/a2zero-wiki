The Electrify Private Fleets initiative provides programmatic support and outreach to help private companies in Ann Arbor transition their fleets to electric vehicles, including working with the State and utilities to expand rebates and incentive programs ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The vision includes ridesharing companies agreeing to franchise agreements requiring at least half of their fleets to be EVs, and medium and large companies encouraging employees to drive EVs while installing workplace charging infrastructure ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The 10-year estimated cost is $123,000 for staffing and administrative costs; GHG reduction potential was not calculated separately but is included within the broader EV bulk buy action ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The initiative targets employees and owners of medium to large businesses in Ann Arbor, with improved local air quality cited as the primary equity benefit ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
