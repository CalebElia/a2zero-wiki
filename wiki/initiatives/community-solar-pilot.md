A community solar pilot was planned for Year Two of A2ZERO, building on Year One work that included negotiating community solar agreements and winning a National Community Solar Partnership technical assistance grant ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]). The pilot is intended to expand solar access to residents who cannot install rooftop solar, including renters and low-income households ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]).
