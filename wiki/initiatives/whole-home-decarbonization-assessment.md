The Whole-Home Decarbonization Assessment is a tool developed in Year Two of A2Zero that provides guidance on energy waste reduction, electrification, and renewable energy installation opportunities ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). The assessment was initially tested in the [[locations/bryant-neighborhood|Bryant neighborhood]] with the goal of expanding city-wide in Year Three ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
