Year Round Composting is projected to reduce 400 MT CO2e at a total cost of $26,000,000 and approximately $65,000 per ton, making it the least cost-effective initiative by cost-per-ton in the A2Zero plan ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Co-benefits include local energy production, job creation, and scalability ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
