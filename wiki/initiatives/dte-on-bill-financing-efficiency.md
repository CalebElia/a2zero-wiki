The A2Zero plan identified DTE Energy on-bill financing for energy efficiency improvements as a potential mechanism to help property owners fund retrofits through their utility bills ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). On-bill financing programs allow customers to repay the cost of efficiency upgrades over time through their monthly utility statements, reducing upfront cost barriers ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
