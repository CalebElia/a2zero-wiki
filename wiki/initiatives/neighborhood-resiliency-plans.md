The A2Zero plan proposes generating neighborhood-based resiliency plans that incorporate residential best practices as a core preparedness action ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Supporting actions include conducting risk assessments and incorporating results into planning, as well as conducting a resilience-aesthetic charrette to integrate community input ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Strengthening relationships with other local governments is also identified as a means of bolstering neighborhood resilience planning ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
