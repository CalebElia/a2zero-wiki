Youth education on carbon neutrality was proposed in the A2Zero plan as an outreach and engagement idea to build climate literacy among young residents ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Engaging youth through educational programming can build long-term community support for climate action and develop the next generation of sustainability advocates ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
