The City of Ann Arbor intervened in Michigan utility case U-20836 to challenge [[actors/dte-energy|DTE Energy]]'s proposed rate increase and proposed changes to compensation structures for customers with solar systems ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). This intervention was undertaken to protect the financial interests of Ann Arbor residents who had invested in residential solar, including those who participated in the [[initiatives/ann-arbor-solarize-program|Ann Arbor Solarize Program]] ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
