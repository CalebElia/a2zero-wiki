Home & Business Electrification is projected to reduce 362,200 MT CO2e, representing 16.5% of total community emissions, at a total cost of $7,100,000 and approximately $20 per ton ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Co-benefits include local energy production, air quality improvement, job creation, and public health improvements ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
