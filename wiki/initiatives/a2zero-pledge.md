The A2ZERO Community Pledge was crafted and launched during Year One of A2ZERO, inviting community members to commit to joining the city in achieving its sustainability goals ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]). The pledge is part of the city's broader community engagement strategy to build public participation in the A2ZERO plan ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]).
