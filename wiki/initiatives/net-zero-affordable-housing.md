Developing net zero affordable housing was identified in the A2Zero plan as an energy efficiency idea that would simultaneously address climate goals and housing equity ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). This concept would ensure that low-income residents benefit from high-performance building standards rather than being excluded from clean energy transitions ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
