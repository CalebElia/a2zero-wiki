The A2Zero plan calls for decentralizing energy infrastructure and incentivizing individual energy production as a resilience measure, alongside creating redundancy in energy systems through storage ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Increasing renewable energy systems, storage, microgrids, and community solar is identified as a related energy resilience action, and planning for higher energy loads due to increasing temperatures is noted as a forward-looking requirement ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Transitioning to underground power lines is also proposed to improve grid reliability ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
