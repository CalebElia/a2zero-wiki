Siting solar energy installations on Ann Arbor's greenbelt lands was identified as a potential energy production idea in the A2Zero Living Carbon Neutrality Plan ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). This concept would leverage publicly protected open land to expand the city's renewable energy capacity ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
