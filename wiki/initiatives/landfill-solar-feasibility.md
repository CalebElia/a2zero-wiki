During Year One of A2ZERO, the city conducted feasibility and cost studies for a 24MW solar installation on a capped landfill ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]). This initiative explores the potential of converting a closed landfill site into a large-scale solar generation facility to support Ann Arbor's renewable energy goals ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]).
