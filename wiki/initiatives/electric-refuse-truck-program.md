The Electric Refuse Truck Program supports the purchase of electric refuse trucks for Ann Arbor's municipal fleet ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). Funding of $270,000 was secured from the American Lung Association and $406,000 from the State of Michigan to support the purchase of two electric refuse trucks ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
