"The Future is Electric" is a joint campaign launched in Year Two of A2Zero in partnership with [[actors/ibew-neca|IBEW/NECA]], the local Electrical Workers Union ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). The campaign aims to bolster electrification readiness among Ann Arbor residents ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
