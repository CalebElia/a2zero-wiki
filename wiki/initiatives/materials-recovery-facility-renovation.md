The A2Zero plan identifies both renovation of the existing city-owned Materials Recovery Facility and development of a new city-owned facility as ideas considered for improving Ann Arbor's recycling infrastructure ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Additional recycling improvements considered include eliminating drop-off fees, implementing multi-stream recycling pickup, expanding neighborhood-level recycling rather than relying on drop-off sites, improving textile recycling, and enforcing recycling compliance ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Public education to resolve recycling uncertainty is also identified as a complementary measure ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
