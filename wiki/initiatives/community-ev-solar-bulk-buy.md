title: Support Community Electric Vehicle and Solar Bulk Buys
type: initiative

The Community EV and Solar Bulk Buy initiative uses group-purchase programs to increase EV accessibility by enabling residents and businesses in Washtenaw County to purchase new and used EVs at a discount of 10–15% ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Programs are offered for a limited time to promote immediate purchases, and are designed to be paired with residential solar installation through the Solarize program to reduce EV charging costs and accelerate the renewable energy transition ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The initiative includes incentives for low-income residents to purchase off-lease or used EVs, and equity provisions explore bulk buys for e-scooters and e-bikes ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The estimated 10-year cost is $700,000, with a GHG reduction potential of 122,900 MTCO2e representing 5.6% of community-wide emissions ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
