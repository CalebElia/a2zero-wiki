Ann Arbor launched a municipal rooftop solar Request for Proposals (RFP) during Year One of A2ZERO, targeting solar installations at thirteen municipal facilities ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]). Year Two plans included completing solar installations at multiple City facilities as a follow-on to this procurement process ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]).
