The City of Ann Arbor applied for and became part of the State of Michigan's NextCycle Intergovernmental Initiative and Public Private Partnership (I2P3) challenge track to boost the circular economy ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). This was accomplished in partnership with many local non-profits as part of the City's broader circular economy strategy under [[strategies/strategy-5-materials-waste|Strategy 5]] ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
