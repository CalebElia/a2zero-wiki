The A2Zero plan proposed developing carbon neutral districts, including zero energy zones and participation in 2030 Districts frameworks, as a place-based strategy for deep decarbonization ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). These district-scale approaches would coordinate building efficiency, electrification, and renewable energy across clusters of properties to achieve net-zero energy outcomes ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
