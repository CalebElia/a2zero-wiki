The Electrification Campaign is a public outreach and education initiative designed to encourage Ann Arbor residents and businesses to switch from fossil fuel-powered equipment to electric alternatives. ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]) Launching and pilot testing the campaign was identified as a Year 3 priority in the A2Zero Year 2 Annual Report.
