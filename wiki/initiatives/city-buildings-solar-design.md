The City of Ann Arbor designed and planned to deploy 4MW of solar photovoltaic systems on municipally owned buildings and infrastructure, with installation identified as a Year 3 priority ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). Targeted facilities include the water treatment plant, water recovery facility, and multiple city parks, with installations planned to begin in summer 2022 ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
