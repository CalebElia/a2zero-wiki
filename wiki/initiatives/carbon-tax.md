Creating a carbon tax was listed among the financing ideas considered in the A2Zero Living Carbon Neutrality Plan as a potential mechanism to price carbon emissions and generate revenue for clean energy programs ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). This concept was included in the appendix of ideas considered but not adopted as a primary A2Zero strategy ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
