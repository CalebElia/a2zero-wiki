type: initiative

The A2Zero plan identifies urban planning measures to moderate heat through shade trees, lighter-colored surfaces, and more rain gardens as a resilience priority ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Street tree actions include analysis of shading and inventory, updating the master plan, and incentivizing private tree planting, while improved parking lot design standards for shading, stormwater retention, heat island mitigation, and trees are also proposed ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Incentivizing white and green roofs is listed as an additional urban heat island mitigation measure ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
