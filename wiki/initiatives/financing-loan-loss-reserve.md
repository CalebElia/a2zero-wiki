The A2Zero plan proposed creating financing opportunities and a loan loss reserve to reduce financial barriers to clean energy investments for residents and businesses ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). A loan loss reserve can de-risk lending for clean energy projects, enabling financial institutions to offer more favorable terms to borrowers who might otherwise be unable to access capital ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
