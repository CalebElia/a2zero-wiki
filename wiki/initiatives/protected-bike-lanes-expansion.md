During Year Two of A2Zero implementation, the city supported the expansion of protected bike lanes as a non-motorized transportation strategy to reduce vehicle miles traveled ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). This effort aligns with [[strategies/strategy-4-vmt-reduction|Strategy 4]] of the A2Zero plan, which targets at least a 50% reduction in vehicle miles traveled ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
