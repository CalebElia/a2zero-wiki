The A2Zero plan proposes scaling community gardens and local food production, expanding farmers markets to year-round and more locations, and developing expanded food hubs and local food in grocery stores ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Collaboration with Ann Arbor Public Schools and the University of Michigan on food planning, purchasing, and waste is also identified as a key action ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
