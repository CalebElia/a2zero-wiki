The A2Zero plan proposes reducing single-use plastics through a combination of organizing a local business cohort, taxing or banning single-use plastics such as bags and takeaway containers, requiring bottle-filling stations, and encouraging price incentives for reusable containers ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). A2Zero-branded compostable alternatives to single-use plastics are also identified as a potential approach ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
