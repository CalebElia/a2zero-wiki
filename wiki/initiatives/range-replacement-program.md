Ann Arbor developed a range replacement program during Year One of A2ZERO to reduce barriers to the adoption of induction ranges through supply and demand side partnerships ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]). The program targets the replacement of gas cooking appliances with electric induction alternatives as part of the broader building electrification strategy ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]).
