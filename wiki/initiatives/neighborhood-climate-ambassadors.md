The A2Zero plan proposes activating neighborhood climate ambassadors and captains to support local resilience efforts and community cohesion ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Complementary actions include activating neighborhood welcome ambassadors and developing a program to recognize neighborhood-level climate and resilience efforts ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Youth participation through actions, videos, ambassadors, curriculum, training, and competitions is also identified as a component of community engagement ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
