The City of Ann Arbor launched a Circular Economy Working Group in Year Two of A2Zero to create a circular economy plan for the City and region, as well as metrics with which to measure it ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). The working group is collaborating with [[actors/iclei|ICLEI-Local Governments for Sustainability]] to establish a series of circular economy metrics for the City of Ann Arbor ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). The City also created a circular economy GIS map including more than 100 circular businesses and organizations in Ann Arbor, with the opportunity for residents to suggest additional circular entities ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
