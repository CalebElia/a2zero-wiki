A regional methane digester was identified in the A2Zero Living Carbon Neutrality Plan as an idea considered for advancing renewable energy production in the Ann Arbor area ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The concept involves capturing methane at a regional scale for energy generation, contributing to both renewable energy goals and waste reduction ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
