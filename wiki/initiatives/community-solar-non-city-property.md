The A2Zero plan identified an opportunity to develop community solar on non-city-owned property, including land owned by the University of Michigan, other municipal entities, and Ann Arbor Public Schools ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). This concept would expand the city's solar capacity by leveraging partner institutions' land assets for shared renewable energy generation ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
