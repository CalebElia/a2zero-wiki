Ann Arbor's EV Charger Deployment initiative expands public electric vehicle charging infrastructure across the city in coordination with multiple institutional partners. ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]) A Year 3 priority was to deploy 50 more EV chargers in coordination with the [[actors/university-of-michigan|University of Michigan]], the [[actors/downtown-development-authority|Downtown Development Authority]], the [[actors/state-of-michigan|State of Michigan]], and other collaborators.
