RE4M is a four-part internal learning series launched during Year One of A2ZERO, focused on how to pair renewable energy, efficiency, electrification, and equity into a program for all Ann Arborites ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]). The series was designed to build internal capacity within the city to deliver integrated, equitable climate programming ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]).
