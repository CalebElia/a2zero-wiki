title: Sustainable Purchasing Policy and Carbon Pricing on Products
type: initiative

The A2Zero plan identifies charging a carbon price on the embedded greenhouse gas emissions of products as a sustainable purchasing strategy ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Additional measures considered include creating a local market for reusable goods, closed-loop reusable packaging, refillable bulk containers and takeout containers at point of sale, and purchasing policies or incentives for municipal operations, institutions, and individuals ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The plan also calls for creating consumption-based greenhouse gas inventories for both the City and local institutions to better track embedded emissions ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
