The A2Zero plan proposes creating and enforcing purchasing policies, guidelines, or incentives for municipal operations, institutions, and individuals, as well as charging a carbon price on the embedded greenhouse gas emissions of products ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Consumption-based inventories for the City and institutions are also identified as supporting actions ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
