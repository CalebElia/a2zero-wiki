A draft Home Energy Rating Disclosure program was advanced during Year Two of A2Zero, with the program expected to be considered by City Council in Year Three ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). The program is part of the City's broader strategy to improve building energy efficiency and transparency for residential properties, and would require disclosure of home energy performance ratings, enabling buyers and renters to make informed decisions about energy costs and efficiency ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
