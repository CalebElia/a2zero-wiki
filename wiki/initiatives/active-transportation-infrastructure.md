type: initiative

The A2Zero plan identifies a suite of active transportation infrastructure improvements including expanding sidewalks and crosswalks in currently un-walkable areas, maintaining and repairing bike lanes, and creating more protected bike lanes and elevated bikeways separated from roads ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The plan also calls for improving snow-clearing to encourage year-round biking and walking, safer lighting for crosswalks and bike lanes, and traffic signals that reflect active transport needs and prioritize safety ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Expanding regional and connected bikeways and improving bike access to downtown are also identified as priorities ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
