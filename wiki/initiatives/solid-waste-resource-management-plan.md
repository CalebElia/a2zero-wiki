The Solid Waste Resource Management Plan (SWRMP) is a foundational planning document referenced across multiple A2Zero Strategy 5 actions, providing estimates and policy frameworks for composting expansion, commercial recycling, and circular economy initiatives ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The SWRMP estimates that year-round composting would add 1,000–2,784 tons of collection capacity, and that expanding food collection from businesses would add 1,000–2,400 tons per year; it also estimates commercial sector recycling diversion at 1,700–4,400 tons annually ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Adoption of the SWRMP was targeted for 2020 as a prerequisite for multiple downstream Strategy 5 actions ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
