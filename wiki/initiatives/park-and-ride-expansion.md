The [[initiatives/park-and-ride-expansion|Increase Number of Park and Rides]] initiative proposes at least a 10x increase in available park and ride spaces from the current approximately 1,300 free spots, with new facilities providing electric vehicle charging and seamless connection to public transit ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The initiative targets a 25% displacement of commuter trips into the City by 2030 and integrates demand management programs such as carpooling and ridesharing ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The 10-year cost is estimated at $170,000,000 (excluding land acquisition costs), with a GHG reduction potential of 28,400 metric tons CO²e, representing 1.3% of community-wide emissions ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Parking at park and rides would remain free to benefit low-income commuters from nearby towns ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
