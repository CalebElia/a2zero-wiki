The Resident Race to Zero is a week-long zero waste challenge that occurs three times per year, engaging Ann Arbor households in reducing their waste ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). As of the Year Two report, 829 households had participated in the A2ZERO Waste Challenge to date ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
