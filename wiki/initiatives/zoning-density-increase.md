Changing zoning laws to allow for greater density was identified in the A2Zero plan's energy education and outreach section as a structural policy idea to reduce per-capita energy use and vehicle miles traveled ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Increased residential and commercial density supports more efficient use of energy infrastructure and reduces dependence on automobile travel ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
