The Enhance Refrigerant Recycling and Reuse Program focuses on enhancing refrigerant recycling programs and discouraging refrigerant use where possible, including banning refrigerants from disposal at City of Ann Arbor landfills to drastically reduce greenhouse gas emissions. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]) The program's success indicator is a 95% reduction in the amount of refrigerants disposed of at City facilities. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]) The 10-year cost is estimated at $179,400; greenhouse gas reduction potential has not been calculated. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]])
