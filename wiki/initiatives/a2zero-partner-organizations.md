The [[actors/office-of-sustainability-and-innovations|Office of Sustainability and Innovations]] sought partner organizations to support outreach, engagement, and eventual implementation of A2Zero; partners were required to co-host a carbon neutrality event, share information with their networks, and display the partner logo on messaging materials ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). As of early March 2020, 67 organizations had signed up as A2Zero partners, spanning environmental nonprofits, civic groups, educational institutions, housing organizations, and local government entities ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). A monthly partner messaging kit was developed and circulated from December through March to make it easy for partners to share A2Zero updates with their networks ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
