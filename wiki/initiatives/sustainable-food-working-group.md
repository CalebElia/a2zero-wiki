The City of Ann Arbor launched a Sustainable Food Working Group in Year Two of A2Zero to help advance sustainable food practices and provide support in the community ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). The working group complements the [[initiatives/sustainable-food-purchasing-policy|Sustainable Food Purchasing Policy]] initiative as part of [[strategies/strategy-5-materials-waste|Strategy 5]] ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
