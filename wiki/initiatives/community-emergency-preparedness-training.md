The A2Zero plan calls for enabling and organizing ongoing emergency preparedness training through the Red Cross, CERT, and other partners, as well as coordinating across Red Cross, AmeriCorps, and additional partner organizations ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The plan also proposes assisting with and organizing the preparation and distribution of residential emergency kits and hosting a National Preparedness Month campaign ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Developing redundant communication systems not dependent on the internet, such as Code Red, is identified as a supporting action ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
