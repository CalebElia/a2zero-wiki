The Avalon Housing Resilience Assessment is a partnership between the City of Ann Arbor and [[actors/avalon-housing|Avalon Housing]] to evaluate and strengthen the climate resilience of affordable housing providers ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). The assessment was initiated in Year Two of A2Zero implementation ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
