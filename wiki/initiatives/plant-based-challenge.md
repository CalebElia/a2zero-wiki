The A2ZERO Plant-Based Challenge was a week-long community event held in Year 3 to encourage the adoption of plant-forward diets ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). The challenge attracted approximately 200 participating residents and over 300 attendees at both in-person and virtual events ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). It was supported by eight local restaurants, six A2ZERO Ambassadors, and seven local nonprofits and governmental departments ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). A Local Food Festival at the Ann Arbor Farmers Market was also held, attended by seventeen local businesses, nonprofits, and community organizations, as well as over 300 residents ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]).
