The A2Zero plan calls for designing roads for flexibility, networked and alternative transportation, slower traffic, stormwater management, trees, boulevards, and durability, with full implementation of green streets as the stated goal ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Minimizing available parking and encouraging and enabling car sharing are listed as complementary transportation actions ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Installation of EV chargers at stores and apartments is also identified as a transportation resilience measure ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
