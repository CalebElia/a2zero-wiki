type: initiative

The A2Zero plan identifies stricter floodway and floodplain management and planning as a water resilience action, alongside enabling and encouraging water generation, reuse, and natural water and flood capture systems ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Adding vegetated buffers along waterways for stormwater management and designing flexible water systems that adapt to conditions are also proposed ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Removing dams and coordinating dam management is listed as an additional water resilience measure ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
