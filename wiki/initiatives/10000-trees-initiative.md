The 10,000 Trees Initiative was developed and launched during Year One of A2ZERO to improve the city's resilience to climate change through green infrastructure ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]). Over 1,000 trees were planted in Year One, with grant funding secured for tree planting in underserved neighborhoods ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]). Year Two plans included launching the BranchOut program and additional FreeTree Giveaways ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]).
