The Energy Concierge and Community Engagement Program creates an easy-to-use service to help residents and businesses understand the best, highest-impact, and most affordable methods to carry out greenhouse gas reduction activities ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The program is described as foundational to the entire carbon neutrality effort, targeting all Ann Arborites, and is intended to continue operating even after carbon neutrality is achieved ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The 10-year cost is estimated at $820,000; the initiative does not directly reduce GHG emissions but enables residents and businesses to find, access, and implement efficiency, renewable energy, and electrification activities outlined in the plan ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). By 2025, the program aims to have helped 2,500 Ann Arborites access energy-related programs and support resources ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
