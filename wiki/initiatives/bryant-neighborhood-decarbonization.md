Ann Arbor launched a decarbonization initiative with the Bryant neighborhood and the [[actors/community-action-network|Community Action Network]] during Year One of A2ZERO ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]). The initiative aims to co-create a whole home health, safety, comfort, and decarbonization assessment with community members, centering equity in the design process ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]).
