title: Bike Lane Expansion and Active Transportation Infrastructure
type: initiative

The plan proposes expanding bike lanes, including protected and elevated bikeways, and increasing regional connected bikeways to improve bike access to downtown ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Sidewalk and crosswalk expansion in currently un-walkable areas, improved snow-clearing, and safer lighting for crosswalks and bike lanes are also proposed ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Enforcing codes protecting bikes and bike lanes from obstructions is identified as a supporting action ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
