Increasing grid capacity to support widespread electrification was identified in the A2Zero plan as a necessary infrastructure investment to enable the transition from fossil fuels to electric systems in buildings and transportation ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). As more buildings and vehicles electrify, the local distribution grid must be upgraded to handle increased electricity demand ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
