This initiative implements the City's existing draft Construction and Demolition Waste plan to ensure 100% of debris is captured and repurposed, with a vision of removing 80% of construction and demolition waste from the landfill ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The action also expands sustainable design requirements into new building approval processes, and would require construction and demolition projects to submit waste and diversion data as part of their building permit or occupancy permit ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Implementation is led by the [[actors/department-of-public-works|Ann Arbor Department of Public Works]] with support from the Planning Department, [[actors/office-of-sustainability-and-innovations|OSI]], and [[actors/resource-recycling-associates|Resource Recycling Associates]] ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The estimated 10-year cost is $270,000; greenhouse gas reduction potential was not calculated ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
