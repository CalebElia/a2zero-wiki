This initiative implements an existing draft City plan for Construction and Demolition Waste to ensure 100% of debris is captured and repurposed, and expands the plan to require sustainable designs in new building approval processes. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]) The goal is to remove 80% of construction and demolition waste from the landfill and support its repurposing for other constructive uses. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]) The 10-year cost is estimated at $270,000; greenhouse gas reduction potential has not been calculated. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]])
