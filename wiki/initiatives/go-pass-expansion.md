The A2Zero plan proposes expanding the Go-Pass area and reducing transit fees or offering annual pass deals to increase ridership ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The plan also considers making public transportation free entirely as a more ambitious option for reducing vehicle miles traveled ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
