The Support a Plant Rich Diet initiative educates the community about the environmental and health benefits of reducing meat consumption and increasing consumption of fruits, vegetables, legumes, whole grains, nuts, and seeds. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]) The initiative includes exploration of a greenhouse gas emissions footprint disclosure on food sold at City facilities, similar to calorie disclosure labeling. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]) The 10-year cost is estimated at $208,400; greenhouse gas reduction potential has not been calculated. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]) The initiative addresses food deserts in the region to help ensure low-income residents have access to healthy, nutritious options. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]])
