The A2Zero plan proposes requiring methane recovery at landfills in active use as part of its resource reduction strategy ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Methane recovery on wastewater is also identified as a cross-cutting action connecting the organics and energy strategies ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
