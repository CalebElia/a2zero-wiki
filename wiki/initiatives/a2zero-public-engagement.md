A2Zero public engagement consisted of three primary components: public events, public surveys, and regular communication via traditional and social media channels ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Staff in the [[actors/office-of-sustainability-and-innovations|Office of Sustainability and Innovations]] hosted 68 public events between November 13, 2019 and March 12, 2020, with a focus on engaging underrepresented constituencies ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Three surveys were administered: the first gathered public ideas, the second collected feedback on preferred emissions-reduction actions and funding approaches, and the third identified what stakeholders needed to support implementation ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
