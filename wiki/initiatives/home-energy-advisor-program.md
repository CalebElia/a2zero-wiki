The A2ZERO Home Energy Advisor Program is a pilot service designed and launched in Year Three of A2ZERO in partnership with [[actors/elevate|Elevate]] and [[actors/ecoworks|EcoWorks]] to assist residents with home electrification ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). The service provides residents with needs assessments, technical advice, contractor engagement, incentives and rebates guidance, bid reviews, and customized paths to reducing greenhouse gas emissions in their homes ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). The program is considered foundational work intended to create scalable new initiatives in Year Four that will help residents and businesses transition to cleaner and healthier ways to heat and power their lives ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]).
