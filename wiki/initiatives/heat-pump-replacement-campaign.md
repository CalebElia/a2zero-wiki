Ann Arbor initiated a heat pump replacement campaign during Year One of A2ZERO to encourage residents to switch from fossil fuel heating to electric heat pumps ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]). The campaign included a sticker program designed to ease the transition process to electric heating systems ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]).
