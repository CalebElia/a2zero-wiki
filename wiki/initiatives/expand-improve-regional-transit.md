The [[initiatives/expand-improve-regional-transit|Expand and Improve Regional Transit]] initiative addresses the approximately 80,000 individuals who commute into Ann Arbor daily for work, school, or recreation, targeting a 25% reduction in commuter trips via regional transit by 2030 ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Priority regional transit corridors identified include Plymouth, Jackson, Detroit, Brighton, Ypsilanti, and Ypsilanti Township ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The initiative also incorporates work-from-home program support, estimating 5% participation for commutes of 10 miles or less and 10% for commutes over 11 miles ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). GHG reduction potential is captured within other actions and not independently quantified; the majority of costs are also accounted for in related actions ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
