The A2Zero plan proposes developing a program to use city buses for evacuation and as power sources during a disaster, linking the transit fleet to emergency response capacity ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Developing climate refugee support networks is identified as a related response action ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
