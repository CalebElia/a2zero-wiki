The [[initiatives/aging-in-place-program|Aging in Place Program]] is an A2Zero initiative designed to support senior residents who wish to remain in familiar neighborhoods by providing access to weatherization programs that lower energy costs and by fostering greater neighborhood social networks. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]) The program reflects A2Zero's equity grounding, ensuring vulnerable populations can thrive amid future climate shocks and stressors.
