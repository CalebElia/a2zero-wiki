Ann Arbor joined a [[actors/world-resources-institute|World Resources Institute (WRI)]] working group composed of peer cities from across the nation to advance 24/7 renewable energy offerings as a pathway to achieving the city's clean energy goals ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). This participation reflects Ann Arbor's engagement in national peer networks to develop innovative clean energy procurement mechanisms beyond standard renewable energy certificates ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
