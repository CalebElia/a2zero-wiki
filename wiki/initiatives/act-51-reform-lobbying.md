title: Act 51 Reform and State Transportation Funding Lobbying
type: initiative

The A2Zero plan proposes lobbying to alter Act 51, the state act for distributing funds for road infrastructure, to give local governments more control over the types of projects funded ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The plan also calls for lobbying for state policy that creates a dedicated funding source for non-motorized and electric transportation, analogous to existing dedicated funding for highways, and for eliminating state registration fees for EVs ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
