The A2Zero plan proposed reducing property taxes for buildings that undergo energy efficiency upgrades as a financial incentive to encourage retrofits ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). This mechanism would use the tax system to lower the effective cost of efficiency improvements for property owners ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
