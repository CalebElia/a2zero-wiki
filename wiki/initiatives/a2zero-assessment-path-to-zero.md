The A2ZERO Assessment: Path to Zero is a planned tool for all Ann Arbor residents, identified as a Year Four priority by the [[actors/office-of-sustainability-and-innovations|Office of Sustainability and Innovations]] ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). The assessment is intended to provide residents with a personalized pathway toward carbon neutrality ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]).
