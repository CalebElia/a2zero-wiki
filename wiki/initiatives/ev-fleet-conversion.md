The A2Zero plan proposes incentivizing and converting fleets to electric vehicles, targeting City fleets, car-sharing services, ride-hailing companies, and commercial delivery operations ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The plan also calls for creating a total cost of ownership target commitment for EVs to guide procurement decisions ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). An ICE vehicle buyback program and encouragement of electric delivery options including autonomous vehicle pilots are also identified as complementary ideas ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
