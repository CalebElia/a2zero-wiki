The Sustaining Ann Arbor Together (SA2T) Grant Program provides funding to community organizations for activities that advance A2ZERO goals ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). The program has an estimated total cost of $1,000,000; GHG reductions were not calculated at the time of plan publication ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Co-benefits include local energy production and resilience ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). In Year 3, over $70,000 was granted through the SA2T program for community climate activities ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]).
