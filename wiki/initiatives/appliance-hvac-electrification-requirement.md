The A2Zero plan proposed requiring electrification of all existing appliances and HVAC systems as a mandatory policy approach to eliminating fossil fuel combustion in buildings ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). This would represent a significant regulatory intervention to accelerate the transition away from gas-powered heating and appliances across Ann Arbor's building stock ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
