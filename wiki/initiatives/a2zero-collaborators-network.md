The A2ZERO Collaborators Network is a partnership network and coalition of organizations designed to expand collaboration between the City and community organizations, businesses, and institutions on Ann Arbor's climate action work ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). By Year Two of A2Zero (2021–2022), the network had expanded to over 110 organizations ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). A Year 3 priority was to further expand the network and unlock greater collaboration opportunities among its members ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
