The A2ZERO Collaborators Network is a partnership network and coalition of organizations designed to expand collaboration between the City and community organizations, businesses, and institutions on Ann Arbor's climate action work, serving as planning and implementation partners for A2ZERO strategies ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]], [[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). By Year Two of A2Zero (2021–2022), the network had expanded to over 110 organizations ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). A Year 3 priority was to further expand the network and unlock greater collaboration opportunities among its members ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]), and by Year 3 the network had grown to over 120 organizations, with [[actors/office-of-sustainability-and-innovations|OSI]] engaging members through virtual and in-person events designed to unlock their potential as partners ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). Expanding and deepening collaboration opportunities with this network is identified as a Year Four priority for the [[actors/office-of-sustainability-and-innovations|Office of Sustainability and Innovations]] ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]).
