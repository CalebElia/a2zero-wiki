The [[initiatives/tiered-parking-rates|Establish Tiered Parking Rates]] initiative creates a sliding parking fee structure that increases costs for general drivers while offering lower rates for low-income residents, residents with disabilities, and electric vehicle drivers, deploying smart metering and dynamic pricing ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Excess on-street parking in the Downtown Development Authority district will be removed and replaced with infrastructure for pedestrians, bicyclists, transit, and street activities such as outdoor restaurant seating ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The initiative also includes eliminating all parking minimums and setting low parking maximums through the plan review process, with a pilot of dynamic parking rates in the DDA planned for 2021 ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The 10-year cost is estimated at $4,500,000, and the GHG reduction potential is captured within other actions rather than independently quantified ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
