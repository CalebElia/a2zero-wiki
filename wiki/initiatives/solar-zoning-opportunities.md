Creating more zoning opportunities for solar energy installations was proposed in the A2Zero plan as a regulatory reform to remove land-use barriers to solar deployment across Ann Arbor ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Zoning changes can enable solar installations in more locations, including on rooftops, parking structures, and open land, accelerating the city's renewable energy transition ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
