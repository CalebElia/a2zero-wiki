[[actors/office-of-sustainability-and-innovations|A2Zero]] continued a bicycle discount program in partnership with local vendors during Year Two of implementation, supporting cycling as an alternative to car travel ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
