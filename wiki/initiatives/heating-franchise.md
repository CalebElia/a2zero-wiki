The Ann Arbor Heating Franchise Initiative is a City-led process to ensure that all Ann Arbor residents have access to safe, healthy, clean, and affordable heating and cooling choices ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). [[actors/office-of-sustainability-and-innovations|OSI]] is leading this work, which includes a new public-facing website, community listening sessions, a request for ideas, and a dedicated email address (franchisefeedback@a2gov.org) to receive public comments ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). The initiative represents a structural intervention in how heating services are delivered and regulated in the city, with equity and clean energy access as central goals ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]).
