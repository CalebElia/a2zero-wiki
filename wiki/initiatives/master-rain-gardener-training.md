The Master Rain Gardener Training initiative supports Ann Arbor residents in installing green infrastructure such as rain gardens to manage stormwater and enhance community resilience ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). City staff undertook this training in Year Two as part of Strategy 6 resilience activities ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
