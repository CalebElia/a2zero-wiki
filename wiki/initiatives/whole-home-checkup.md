A whole home check-up program was planned for Year Two of A2ZERO to help individual residents understand their efficiency, electrification, and renewable energy options ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]). This initiative builds on Year One work including the Bryant neighborhood decarbonization assessment and the IBEW 252 home electrification assessment collaboration ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]).
