type: initiative

The A2Zero plan proposes increasing natural area preservation and stewardship for carbon sequestration and stormwater management, as well as connecting green spaces across the city ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Additional green space actions include increasing land use for local food production, hosting a food infrastructure innovation challenge, and encouraging fruit trees, foraging, and food production on city lands in neighborhoods ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Purchasing repetitive loss properties and redeveloping vacant space as green space is also identified as an urban planning measure ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
