The Washington Street Bike Boulevard is a planned protected bicycle infrastructure project in Ann Arbor, funded by a $200,000 SEMCOG Carbon Reduction Program grant ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). Construction is scheduled to begin in fall 2025 as part of the City's broader effort to expand protected bike infrastructure ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). The project supports A2Zero's vehicle miles traveled reduction goals by expanding safe, low-carbon transportation options for residents ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]).
