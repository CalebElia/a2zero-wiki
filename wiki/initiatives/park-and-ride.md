The Park and Ride initiative is projected to reduce 28,400 MT CO2e, representing 1.3% of total community emissions, at a total cost of $170,000,000 and approximately $5,986 per ton ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Co-benefits include air quality improvement, equity benefits, and scalability ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
