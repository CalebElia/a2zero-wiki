The Resident Race to Zero Energy was Ann Arbor's first residential energy reduction competition, launched and concluded during Year 3 of A2ZERO ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). The competition attracted over 100 participants and was designed to motivate residents to reduce their home energy consumption ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]).
