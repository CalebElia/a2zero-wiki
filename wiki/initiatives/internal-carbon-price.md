The Establish Internal Carbon Price initiative creates a financial mechanism whereby all City Service Areas integrate greenhouse gas emissions into decision-making through an internal carbon tax — a per-ton charge on emissions that is recycled back into energy efficiency improvements and renewable energy installations at City facilities ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The program is designed to pilot at $5/ton in 2020 and increase to $10/ton in 2021, with the price capped at no more than the social cost of carbon ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Funds generated by enterprise funds are assumed to remain available only for use by those enterprise funds, and all funds generated must be applied to energy efficiency or renewable energy activities ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The 10-year estimated cost is $75,000, and greenhouse gas reduction potential was not calculated for this initiative ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
