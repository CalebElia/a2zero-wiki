Ann Arbor initiated a collaboration with IBEW Local 252 during Year One of A2ZERO to create a home electrification assessment tool ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]). This partnership connects the city's electrification goals with organized labor, leveraging electricians' expertise to evaluate residential electrification readiness ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]).
