The Anti-Idling Campaign is a collaborative effort between the City of Ann Arbor and [[actors/ann-arbor-public-schools|Ann Arbor Public Schools]] to reduce vehicle idling near schools ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). The initiative was underway during Year Two of A2Zero (2021–2022) ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
