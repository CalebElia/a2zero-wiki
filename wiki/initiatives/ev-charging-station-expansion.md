type: initiative

The plan proposes requiring new construction to include EV charging stations and expanding existing charging infrastructure in low-demand areas, car-sharing, ride-hailing, and Park and Ride lots ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). EV charging stations, both private and public, would be paired with solar power and storage where possible ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Subsidies for EVs and charging are proposed specifically for low-income households ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The plan also calls for coordinating a Workplace Charging Challenge with [[actors/dte-energy|DTE Energy]] and leveraging DTE rebates ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
