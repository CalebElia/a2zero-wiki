Detroit is the largest city in southeast Michigan and a key regional transit destination in A2ZERO's long-range transit expansion plans. The A2ZERO plan targets high-capacity transit connecting Ann Arbor with Detroit by 2030 through the [[actors/regional-transit-authority-of-southeast-michigan|Regional Transit Authority of Southeast Michigan]] ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
