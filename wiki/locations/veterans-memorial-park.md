[[locations/veterans-memorial-park|Veterans Memorial Park]] in Ann Arbor was used as an A2Zero outreach tabling location on January 8, 2020, during a High School Hockey game, as part of the broader public engagement campaign ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Veterans Park is also a City of Ann Arbor park identified as a site for geothermal district planning in Year 3 of A2ZERO ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). Clean Energy to Communities technical assistance was secured to identify data needs and draft a request for proposals for a geothermal design around Veterans Park ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]).
