The City of Ann Arbor's former landfill is the primary site for the Landfill Solar Project, a utility-scale solar installation targeting 24MW of capacity by 2023 ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The project also includes a pilot of floating solar panels installed on a retention pond at the site, as well as pollinator habitat creation as a co-benefit ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). If enabling legislation is passed and community demand exists, the landfill solar site is planned to open for community solar subscriptions in 2026 ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
