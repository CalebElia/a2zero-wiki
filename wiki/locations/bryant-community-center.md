[[locations/bryant-community-center|Bryant Community Center]] in Ann Arbor is a City of Ann Arbor community facility that served as the site of a joint A2Zero and Ann Arbor Housing Commission community outreach event on February 19, 2020, as part of the broader A2Zero public engagement campaign ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The center also functions as an AQMesh air quality monitoring site and a planned Resilience Hub ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). The design for the 2nd Resilience Hub at this location was completed in Year Two, with implementation planned for Year 3 ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
