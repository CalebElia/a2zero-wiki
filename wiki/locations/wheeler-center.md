The Wheeler Center is a City of Ann Arbor facility located at the site of the former Ann Arbor landfill, designated as the location for the planned 20MW [[initiatives/wheeler-center-solar-park|Wheeler Center Solar Park]] ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). It is also the site of the first community solar offering secured in DTE Energy's service territory through the settlement of utility case U-20713 ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). Breaking ground on the Wheeler Center Solar Park was listed as a Year 3 priority in the A2Zero Year 2 Annual Report ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).

The [[locations/wheeler-center|Wheeler Service Center]] hosted a Climate Reality Presentation on December 20, 2019, reaching approximately 140 city staff members and including an introduction to the A2Zero initiative ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
