The [[locations/ann-arbor-senior-center|Ann Arbor Senior Center]] is a City of Ann Arbor facility designated as the 3rd planned Resilience Hub under the A2Zero Resilience Hubs initiative, with design completed in Year Two and implementation planned for Year 3 ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
