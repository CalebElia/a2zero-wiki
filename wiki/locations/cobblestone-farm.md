[[locations/cobblestone-farm|Cobblestone Farm]] is a city-owned facility in Ann Arbor that served as the site of the public launch of the [[initiatives/a2zero-public-engagement-process|A2Zero initiative]] on November 13, 2019, with over 250 individuals in attendance ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). It was also the location of the Homegrown at The Cobblestone Art Show in December 2019, where A2Zero staff tabled to share information about the plan ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
