The Bryant neighborhood in Ann Arbor was selected as the site for a neighborhood-scale decarbonization initiative during Year One of A2ZERO, in partnership with the Community Action Network ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]). The initiative focuses on co-creating whole home health, safety, comfort, and decarbonization assessments with residents ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]).
