Washtenaw County is the county jurisdiction containing Ann Arbor and Ypsilanti, located in southeast Michigan. Several A2ZERO programs extend to county-wide partnerships and beneficiaries, particularly for community solar, weatherization, and transit initiatives ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
