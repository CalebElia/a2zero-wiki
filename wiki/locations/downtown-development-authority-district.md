The [[locations/downtown-development-authority-district|Downtown Development Authority District]] is the primary site for the [[initiatives/tiered-parking-rates|Establish Tiered Parking Rates]] initiative, where excess on-street parking will be removed and replaced with infrastructure for pedestrians, bicyclists, transit, and street activities such as outdoor restaurant seating ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). A pilot of dynamic parking rates is planned for somewhere within the DDA district in 2021, with full-scale launch of tiered rates by 2022 ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The DDA is also a collaborator on the [[initiatives/non-motorized-transportation-plan|Non-Motorized Transportation Plan]] and [[initiatives/park-and-ride-expansion|Park and Ride Expansion]] initiatives ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
