The Ann Arbor Greenbelt is a network of permanently protected open lands surrounding Ann Arbor, preserved through the City's Greenbelt Millage program. The A2ZERO plan identified greenbelt lands as a potential site for solar energy installations, noting that publicly protected open land could expand the city's renewable energy capacity ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
