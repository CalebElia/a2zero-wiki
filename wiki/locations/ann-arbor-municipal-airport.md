The Ann Arbor Municipal Airport is one of the City facilities selected for solar installation under the ARPA City Facilities Solar initiative, with vendors Enerlogics and Melink Solar selected for roughly 3 MW of combined solar across this and other municipal sites, with construction set to begin fall 2023 ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]).
