The Ann Arbor capped landfill is a closed municipal solid waste facility owned by the City of Ann Arbor. During Year One of A2ZERO, the city commissioned a feasibility and cost study for a 24MW solar installation on the site, exploring the potential to convert the capped landfill into a large-scale renewable energy generation facility ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]).
