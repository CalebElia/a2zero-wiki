The Ann Arbor Farmers Market is a city-owned outdoor market in the Kerrytown district. It served as one of several community venues where the [[actors/office-of-sustainability-and-innovations|Office of Sustainability and Innovations]] hosted A2ZERO public engagement events during the 2019–2020 plan development period ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The market was also the planned venue for the A2Zero Electrification Expo on July 15, 2022, bringing together vendors, local businesses, live music, and food trucks in support of electrification ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). Additionally, the market was selected as one of two initial sites for AQMesh air quality monitor installation under the A2Zero air quality monitoring initiative ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
