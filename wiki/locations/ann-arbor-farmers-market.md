The Ann Arbor Farmers Market is a city-owned outdoor market in the Kerrytown district. It served as one of several community venues where the [[actors/office-of-sustainability-and-innovations|Office of Sustainability and Innovations]] hosted A2ZERO public engagement events during the 2019–2020 plan development period ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
