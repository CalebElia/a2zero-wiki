Ypsilanti is a city in Washtenaw County adjacent to Ann Arbor. It is a key destination in A2ZERO's regional transit expansion plans — the [[actors/ann-arbor-area-transit-authority|Ann Arbor Area Transit Authority]] serves the Ann Arbor–Ypsilanti corridor, and A2ZERO's 2030 target includes significantly increasing transit ridership between the two cities ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
