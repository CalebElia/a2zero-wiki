Induction ranges are electric cooking appliances that Ann Arbor is promoting as a replacement for gas stoves through its range replacement program under A2ZERO ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]). The program addresses both supply and demand side barriers to adoption, recognizing that cost and availability are key obstacles to residential electrification of cooking ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]).
