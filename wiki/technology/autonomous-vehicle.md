The A2Zero plan encourages pilot programs for autonomous and connected vehicles, including electric delivery options, as part of the motor vehicle electrification strategy ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The plan identifies supporting infrastructure including 5G, DSRC, and WiFi as enabling technologies for connected and autonomous vehicles ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Autonomous delivery vehicles are also envisioned as one component of the proposed mobility hubs concept ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
