Solar PV is central to three of Ann Arbor's four Strategy 1 actions, with deployment planned across residential rooftops, commercial buildings, public properties, and the City-owned landfill. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]) Upfront installation costs are identified as a primary barrier, addressed through bulk purchase discounts of 5–15% and on-bill financing for community solar subscribers. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]) The landfill project also pilots floating solar panels on a retention pond. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]])
