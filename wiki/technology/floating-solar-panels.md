The Landfill Solar Project includes a pilot installation of solar panels on a retention pond at the City-owned landfill site, representing Ann Arbor's first deployment of floating solar (floatovoltaic) technology ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). This pilot is part of a broader 24MW utility-scale solar project developed in partnership with [[actors/dte-energy|DTE Energy]] and [[actors/pittsfield-township|Pittsfield Township]] ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
