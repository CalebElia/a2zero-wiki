District geothermal systems use shared ground loop infrastructure to provide heating and cooling to multiple buildings, achieving greater efficiency than individual installations ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The A2Zero plan identified geothermal districts as a potential energy efficiency and electrification strategy for Ann Arbor, though no specific sites or implementation details were specified in this section ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
