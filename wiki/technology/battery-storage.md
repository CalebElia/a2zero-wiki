Solar-powered battery storage was deployed at the Northside Community Center as part of Ann Arbor's first A2ZERO resilience hub, providing up to three days of backup power in case of a grid outage ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]). This deployment demonstrates the use of paired solar and storage systems to enhance community resilience in underserved neighborhoods ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year1|a2zero-year1]]).
