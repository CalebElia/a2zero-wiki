The State of Michigan awarded a $54,000 grant to the City of Ann Arbor in Year 3 of A2ZERO to install the City's first curbside EV chargers, representing the state's first utility pole EV charging program ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). Installation of these utility-pole-mounted chargers was targeted for fall 2023 ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). This curbside charging infrastructure represents a novel deployment model for Michigan ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]).
