The City of Ann Arbor and Community Action Network won a $500,000 MI-HOPE grant in Year 3 of A2ZERO to advance neighborhood decarbonization in the Bryant neighborhood ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). The grant is supporting 19 lower-income households in making health, safety, and quality of life improvements as part of the broader [[initiatives/bryant-neighborhood-decarbonization|Bryant Neighborhood Decarbonization Initiative]] ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]).
