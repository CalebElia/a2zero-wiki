Washtenaw County provided a $4,000 Waste Reduction Sponsorship to advance the A2R3 returnable containers program in Year 3 ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). This funding supports the [[initiatives/a2r3-reduce-reuse-return|A2R3 Reduce Reuse Return]] initiative's work with local restaurants to maintain reusable container programs ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]).
