The Urban Sustainability Directors Network awarded a $15,200 mini-grant to the City of Ann Arbor to support the Aging in Place Efficiently (AIP) program ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). The AIP program completes energy efficiency and aging-in-place retrofits for lower-income homeowners ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]).
