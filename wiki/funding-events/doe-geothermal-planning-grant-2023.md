The U.S. Department of Energy awarded a $580,000 Community Geothermal Planning Grant to Ann Arbor in Year 3 to design a district geothermal system in the Bryant neighborhood ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). This grant supports the [[initiatives/geothermal-districts|Geothermal Districts]] initiative and is a key component of the [[initiatives/bryant-neighborhood-decarbonization|Bryant Neighborhood Decarbonization Initiative]] ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]).
