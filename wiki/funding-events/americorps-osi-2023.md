A $229,000 AmeriCorps grant was secured by the [[actors/office-of-sustainability-and-innovations|Office of Sustainability and Innovations]] in Year Three of A2ZERO to bring 10 AmeriCorps members to OSI in support of local sustainability-related community engagement and education programs ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). This grant directly supports the expansion of the [[initiatives/a2zero-ambassadors-program|A2ZERO Ambassadors Program]] and related community outreach activities ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]).
