The City of Ann Arbor secured $2.5 million in federal support through the American Rescue Plan Act to advance solar and battery storage installations on critical City facilities ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). Following the award, a request for proposals was released and vendors Enerlogics and Melink Solar were selected for installation of roughly 3 MW of solar on municipal sites including Wheeler Service Center, the Water and Wastewater Treatment Plants, and the municipal airport ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]).
