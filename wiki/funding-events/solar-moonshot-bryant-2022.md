Solar Moonshot awarded $25,000 to support the installation of solar panels on two homes in Ann Arbor's Bryant neighborhood ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). This grant was secured during Year Two of A2Zero (2021–2022) as part of the [[initiatives/bryant-neighborhood-decarbonization|Bryant Neighborhood Decarbonization]] initiative ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
