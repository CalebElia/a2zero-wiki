In Year Three of A2ZERO, the City of Ann Arbor, together with Public Works and Natural Areas Preservation, submitted a USDA Forest Service Urban & Community Forestry Inflation Reduction Act grant proposal ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]). The proposed grant would fund an update to Ann Arbor's Urban Tree Canopy assessment, advance private property tree planting efforts, create a Natural Areas Preservation management plan, and conduct a feasibility study for a City- and County-owned tree nursery ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year3|a2zero-year3]]).
