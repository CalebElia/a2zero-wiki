The State of Michigan awarded $406,000 to support the purchase of an electric refuse truck for Ann Arbor's municipal fleet ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]). This grant was secured during Year Two of A2Zero (2021–2022) as part of the [[initiatives/electric-refuse-truck-program|Electric Refuse Truck Program]] ([[sources/annual-reports/a2zero-year2|a2zero-year2]]).
