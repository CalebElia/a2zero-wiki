Hard Rules
✓ Synthesis pages cite wiki pages
Synthesis pages may cite strategies, initiatives, commitments, meetings, actors — any primary wiki type. Provenance chains back to Silver + Bronze through those pages.
✗ No synthesis-to-synthesis citations
Never cite another synthesis page as a source. Chain contamination: if Synthesis A is wrong, everything derived from it inherits the error. All synthesis must trace directly to primary wiki types.
⚠ Synthesis stays out of quads
Synthesis pages are not ingested into quads.jsonl. They're derived artifacts, not source facts. Quads stay clean — only directly extracted from Silver source documents.
→ Low-quality synthesis = dark matter signal
When the LLM can't synthesize a good answer ("insufficient sources"), that's a wiki gap. Flag it: the question itself becomes a dark matter dossier entry — a signal to go find more Bronze.
The compounding asset: Synthesis pages sorted by retrieval-count are your FAQ — a map of what stakeholders most want to understand about the program. Over time this tells Grapevine which mechanisms people are hungriest to understand, and those are the ones most worth formalizing into mechanism pages for the replication playbook.

The staleness trigger runs automatically after every ingest: scan new quads for entity matches against invalidation-triggers across all synthesis pages → flip matching pages to needs-review. A synthesis page about geothermal that was accurate in January automatically surfaces for review when the Bryant federal funding freeze is ingested in February. The knowledge base stays honest without manual tracking.
