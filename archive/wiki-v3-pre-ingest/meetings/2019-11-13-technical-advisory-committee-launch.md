On November 13, 2019, four Technical Advisory Committee meetings were hosted simultaneously to launch the TAC element of the A2Zero planning process ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The four committees established were: Mobility, Energy, Resource Reduction, and Adaptation and Resilience ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
