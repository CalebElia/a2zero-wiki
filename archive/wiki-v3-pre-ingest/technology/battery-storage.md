Battery storage is included as a component of the Onsite Renewables Bulk Buy program, with a goal that at least one-quarter of the 78MW of new installations also include some form of battery storage by 2030. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]) The inclusion of battery storage is intended to enhance the resilience of the local community and economy. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]])
