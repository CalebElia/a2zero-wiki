Electric vehicles are a central technology in the A2Zero mobility strategy, with proposals covering fleet conversion, individual bulk purchases, and subsidies for low-income households ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The plan proposes pairing EV charging stations with solar power and storage, and coordinating a Workplace Charging Challenge with [[actors/dte-energy|DTE Energy]] ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). A buyback program for ICE vehicles and a battery recycling program for utility batteries to be used in school and public buses are also proposed ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
