Ann Arbor is the Michigan city implementing the A2Zero Living Carbon Neutrality Plan, with a community-wide goal of achieving carbon neutrality by 2030. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]) The city has experienced locally observable climate impacts including increased precipitation, rising temperatures, and greater weather volatility. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]])

<!-- source: cap-2020 -->
Ann Arbor is the primary geographic scope of the A2Zero Living Carbon Neutrality Plan, which targets community-wide carbon neutrality by 2030 ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The entire Ann Arbor community emitted 2.1 million metric tons of CO2e in 2018, primarily from natural gas, electricity, gasoline, coal, and diesel used by residents, visitors, students, and businesses ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).

Ann Arbor is the Michigan city implementing the A2Zero carbon neutrality plan, with community-wide greenhouse gas emissions of 2.1 million metric tons as of 2018 ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The city's average annual temperature has already increased nearly 1°F since the 1900s, and temperatures are projected to rise by 3–7°F more by 2050 ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Average annual precipitation has increased by 44% since the 1950s, and the total rain falling during extreme events has increased by 37% since 1981 ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).

<!-- source: cap-2020 -->
Ann Arbor is the primary geographic scope of the A2Zero community-wide carbon neutrality plan, targeting carbon neutrality by 2030 ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The city's plan encompasses residents, businesses, schools, places of worship, and recreational sites across the community ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).

<!-- source: cap-2020 -->
Ann Arbor is the primary jurisdiction for the A2Zero Living Carbon Neutrality Plan and the target community for achieving carbon neutrality by 2030 ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The Ann Arbor City Council adopted the original Climate Action Plan in 2012 and in 2019 declared a climate emergency, committing to community-wide carbon neutrality ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
