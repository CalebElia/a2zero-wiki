type: location

The City-owned landfill and surrounding property, including land held in a PUD with Pittsfield Township, is the designated site for Ann Arbor's 24MW utility-scale Landfill Solar Project. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]) The project includes ground-mounted solar, a floating solar pilot on a retention pond, and pollinator habitat. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]) If demand exists and enabling legislation passes, the site may be opened for community solar subscriptions. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]])
