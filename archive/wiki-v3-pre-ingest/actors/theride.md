[[actors/theride|TheRide]] is the Ann Arbor Area Transportation Authority, the public transit provider serving the Ann Arbor region ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The A2Zero plan identifies expanding TheRide bus routes and electrifying TheRide vehicles as key mobility actions ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
