DTE Energy is a collaborator on both the Community Solar Program and the Landfill Solar Project under A2Zero Strategy 1. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]) For the Community Solar Program, the City plans to work with DTE Energy to create a local pilot program once enabling state legislation is passed. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]) For the Landfill Solar Project, DTE Energy is a development partner on the utility-scale installation at the City-owned landfill and Pittsfield Township land. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]])

<!-- source: cap-2020 -->
[[actors/dte-energy|DTE Energy]] is identified in the A2Zero plan as a partner for coordinating a Workplace Charging Challenge and leveraging DTE rebates to support EV charging expansion ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). DTE is listed among the motor vehicle electrification ideas considered for the A2Zero mobility strategy ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
