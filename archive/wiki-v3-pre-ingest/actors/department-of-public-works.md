The Department of Public Works is the party responsible for implementing multiple A2Zero Strategy 5 actions, including expanding composting, expanding commercial recycling, requiring sustainable materials in developments, moving toward a circular economy, and enhancing refrigerant recycling. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]) The department works in collaboration with the [[actors/office-of-sustainability-and-innovations|Office of Sustainability and Innovations]] and external partners such as [[actors/resource-recycling-associates|Resource Recycling Associates]] across these initiatives. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]])
