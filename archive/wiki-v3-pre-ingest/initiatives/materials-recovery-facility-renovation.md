The A2Zero plan identifies both renovation of the existing city-owned Materials Recovery Facility and development of a new city-owned facility as potential recycling infrastructure actions ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Additional recycling improvements proposed include eliminating drop-off fees, multi-stream pickup, neighborhood-level recycling, and improved textile recycling ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
