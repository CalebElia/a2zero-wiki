The A2Zero plan proposes scaling pledge programs including the Blue Zone Program to encourage plant-based diets, incorporating Ann Arbor Public Schools and other institutions ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Cooking classes focused on plant-based, local, and seasonal foods through Recreation and Education are also identified as a supporting action ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
