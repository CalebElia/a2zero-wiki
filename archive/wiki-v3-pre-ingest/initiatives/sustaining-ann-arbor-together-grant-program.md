The [[initiatives/sustaining-ann-arbor-together-grant-program|Sustaining Ann Arbor Together Grant Program]] provides small grants to residents, schools, local nonprofits, and local businesses to advance sustainability-related activities in Ann Arbor's right of way. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]) The program assumes $100,000 will continue to be available annually and targets at least 10 grants awarded per year. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]) The 10-year estimated cost is $1,000,000, and greenhouse gas reduction potential was not calculated. ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]])
