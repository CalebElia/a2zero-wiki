The A2Zero plan identifies creation of a Zero Waste Plan as a key action under resource reduction, intended to guide citywide waste minimization efforts ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Related ideas include requiring all city-wide events to be zero waste and reporting out on what happens to recovered waste ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
