The [[actors/office-of-sustainability-and-innovations|OSI]] created a meeting-in-a-box kit that included materials enabling individuals to host their own A2Zero events ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). The toolkit included a hosting guide, a slide deck with talking points and discussion questions, a terminology sheet, an A2Zero overview sheet, a climate change overview document, a sign-in sheet, links to A2Zero surveys, an event results form, trivia questions, and a photo release form ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
