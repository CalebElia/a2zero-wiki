The plan proposes expanding [[actors/theride|TheRide]] bus routes, connecting routes more effectively, and increasing the speed and efficiency of bus transit with rapid transit along major corridors ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Electrifying TheRide vehicles and right-sizing transit vehicles are also proposed actions ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]). Making public transportation free and expanding the Go-Pass area are identified as fare-related strategies to increase ridership ([[sources/cap/cap-2020|cap-2020]]).
