## What it is

A consumer-facing campaign to encourage homeowners to replace fossil-fuel
heating systems with electric heat pumps. Year 1 details include a sticker
program designed to ease the transition process — likely a visible
social-proof mechanism (stickers on homes that have made the switch) to
normalize the technology and reduce perceived risk for neighbors.

## Year 1 (a2zero-year1)

Campaign initiated with a sticker program component (a2zero-year1,
Strategy 2). No adoption numbers or homes-transitioned count reported
in Year 1 — this was a launch year.

## Year 2 (a2zero-year2)

Not mentioned by name in the Year 2 report. Multiple heat-pump-adjacent
activities occurred (contractor training and badging, "Future is Electric"
campaign with IBEW/NECA, Electrification Expo), suggesting the heat pump
campaign may have been absorbed into broader electrification programming
rather than continuing as a standalone named effort.
