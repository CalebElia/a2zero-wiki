## What it is

A city-wide conversion of streetlights to LEDs, reducing energy consumption
and operational costs. Funded primarily by a $980,000 SEMCOG Carbon Reduction
Program grant, with construction starting fall 2023. A smaller $25,000 State
of Michigan grant covers LED conversion at Wheeler Service Center specifically.

## Year 3 (a2zero-year3)

Grant won; conversion beginning fall 2023. OSI also submitted testimony in
DTE utility case U-20836 calling for improved streetlighting services and
rates — linking utility policy and the physical conversion work.

## Status as of Year 3

Funded; implementation beginning. Outcome data (energy savings, cost
reduction, number of fixtures converted) to be reported in Year 4.
