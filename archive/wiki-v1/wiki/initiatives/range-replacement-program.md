## What it is

A program to reduce barriers to adoption of induction (electric) ranges,
operating on both supply side (working with vendors to make induction ranges
available) and demand side (reducing cost or friction for homeowners
switching from gas). Framed as reducing barriers rather than mandating
transitions.

## Year 1 (a2zero-year1)

Program developed and launched (a2zero-year1, Strategy 2). No adoption
numbers reported in Year 1.

## Year 2 (a2zero-year2)

Not mentioned by name in the Year 2 report. Like the Heat Pump Replacement
Campaign, this program may have been subsumed into broader electrification
programming. The permitting system update to track appliance transitions
(including stoves) that was reported in Year 2 could be downstream
infrastructure for this program.

Unknown — name absent from Year 2 reporting. Flag for Year 3.
