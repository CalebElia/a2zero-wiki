## What it is

A platform providing Ann Arbor residents an easy way to obtain design
estimates and pricing for replacing old furnaces with new heat pumps,
including identification of all applicable rebates, plus contractor and
installation oversight. Complements the Home Energy Rebate program (which
funds the installations) and the Home Energy Advisor (which provides the
initial assessment).

## Year 5 (a2zero-year5)

Launched in Year 5. Designed to lower the friction of the furnace-to-heat
pump transition by bundling the design, pricing, and rebate identification
steps into a single platform — one of the key barriers to heat pump adoption
has been complexity of the contractor selection and incentive-stacking process.

## Status as of Year 5

Active. Launched Year 5.
