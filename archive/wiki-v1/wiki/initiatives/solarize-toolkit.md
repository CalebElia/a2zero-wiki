## What it is

A replication guide for the Ann Arbor Solarize model — documenting the
approach so other communities can implement group-purchasing residential
solar programs beyond Washtenaw County. Developed for and distributed
through the Great Lakes Renewable Energy Association (GLREA).

## Year 1 (a2zero-year1)

Launched this year: described as "a step-by-step guide to implementing the
Solarize model in other communities" (a2zero-year1, Strategy 1). This is the
first formal packaging of the Ann Arbor residential solar group-purchasing
model for external replication.

## Year 2 (a2zero-year2)

Toolkit formally developed and delivered to GLREA (a2zero-year2, Strategy 1).
Also in Year 2: Ann Arbor Solar Stories was launched with GLREA to enable
Ann Arbor residents to serve as peer advocates for residential solar adoption.

## Year 3 (a2zero-year3)

An updated Toolkit was published and OSI led state-wide, regional, and
national engagement around the Solarize Ann Arbor model — including
conference invitations and a GLREA Exemplary Partnership Award for OSI
staff. The Toolkit is now in its second iteration (a2zero-year3, Strategy 7).

## Status as of Year 3

Delivered. The toolkit's reach — how many communities have adopted the
Solarize model using it — is not tracked in the annual report series but
would be worth querying GLREA for in any external research.
