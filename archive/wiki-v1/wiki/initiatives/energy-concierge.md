## What it is

A one-on-one service co-designed with community partners to help residents
create prescriptive roadmaps toward home improvements and decarbonization —
covering energy waste reduction, electrification, and renewable energy
installation. Described in both Strategy 2 and Strategy 3 sections of
Year 2, reflecting its cross-cutting nature.

## Year 2 (a2zero-year2)

Co-design with community partners was underway; the service was gearing
up to be tested in Year 3. A whole-home decarbonization assessment tool
was developed and piloted in the Bryant neighborhood this year as a
precursor — see [[../topics/bryant-neighborhood-decarbonization|Bryant Neighborhood Decarbonization]].

## Year 3 (a2zero-year3)

Designed and currently piloting as the "Home Energy Advisor" service with
partners Elevate and EcoWorks. Delivers needs assessments, technical advice,
contractor engagement, incentive and rebate guidance, bid reviews, and
customized decarbonization paths for residents (a2zero-year3, Strategy 2).
The name changed from "Energy Concierge" to "Home Energy Advisor" between
Year 2 and Year 3.

Year 4 priority is "Fully launching the A2ZERO Home Energy Advisor Program."

## Year 4 (a2zero-year4)

Fully launched city-wide. "Launched the A2ZERO Home Energy Advisor service
to help residents determine their next best step on the path to
decarbonization. This free service is available to anyone in the City of
Ann Arbor." Separate from the Home Energy Rebate program (Elevate contract),
which designs and funds specific improvements; the Home Energy Advisor is
the intake/planning step before rebates are applied for.

## Status as of Year 4

Launched. Available to all Ann Arbor residents. Fulfills
[[../commitments/y4-home-energy-advisor-launch|Year 3 commitment]].
