## What it is

A national AmeriCorps program focused on climate education and behavior
change in Ann Arbor. Ten AmeriCorps members were funded with a $229K grant
secured in Year 3, with the program formally launching in Year 4.

## Year 3 (a2zero-year3)

$229K AmeriCorps grant secured to launch 10-member program. Program designed
but not yet operational at time of Year 3 report publication.

## Year 4 (a2zero-year4)

Launched. Ten AmeriCorps members working on education and behavior change
across the community. Named the "Ann Arbor Climate Corps" in the report.
Positioned as both a community engagement mechanism and a workforce
development pathway for climate jobs.

## Status as of Year 4

Active. 10 members. AmeriCorps framework. $229K grant.
