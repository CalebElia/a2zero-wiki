## What it is

A program to help Ann Arbor businesses reduce their environmental footprint
through practical sustainability improvements. Launched in partnership with
SPARK Ann Arbor, open to all local businesses.

## Year 3 (a2zero-year3)

Design work underway; mentioned as being developed "with SPARK."

## Year 4 (a2zero-year4)

Formally launched. Open to all Ann Arbor businesses. Partnership with SPARK
provides business development support alongside sustainability coaching.
Year 5 target: scale to 50 local businesses — see
[[../commitments/y5-green-business-challenge-50|Year 5 commitment]].

## Status as of Year 4

Active. Launched Year 4. Year 5 target: 50 enrolled businesses.
