## What it is

A program to train contractors on electrification opportunities in Michigan
and certify ("badge") qualified professionals. Residents can find vetted
contractors through the Michigan Saves Locator. Funded in part by a $8,500
grant from EGLE (a2zero-year2, Strategy 7 grants).

## Year 2 (a2zero-year2)

Launched this year. The program addresses a key demand-side barrier to
electrification: even willing homeowners face difficulty finding contractors
who understand heat pump installation, panel upgrades, and EV charger
installation. By credentialing contractors, the program creates a vetted
supply side to match growing consumer interest.

## Year 3 (a2zero-year3)

22 contractors statewide now hold the Michigan Saves Electrification Badge —
the first measurable output. OSI held a convening of 30+ contractors across
multiple disciplines to discuss electrification misconceptions, City programs,
and upcoming rebate program design. The badge program was created by Michigan
Saves with City assistance.

## Status as of Year 3

Active. 22 badged contractors statewide. Contractor network engaged in
rebate program co-design for Year 4.
