## What it is

A program aimed at expanding solar access for low-to-moderate income (LMI)
households. Referenced in Year 1 strategy synthesis as backed by a National
Community Solar Partnership grant for technical assistance.

## Year 1 (a2zero-year1)

Development began. National Community Solar Partnership grant won for
technical assistance.

## Year 2 (a2zero-year2)

Not explicitly mentioned in Year 2 report. The utility settlement in U-20713
included reduced rates for MIGreenPower enrollees, which has some overlap
with LMI goals but is not an LMI-specific program. The Year 1 commitment to
create a new LMI solar program remains unverified as of Year 2 — check Year 3.

## Status as of Year 2

Unverified. May have been folded into other initiatives or may be proceeding
without a distinct name in reporting. Flag for Year 3 check.
