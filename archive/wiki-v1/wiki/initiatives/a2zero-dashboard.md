## What it is

A public dashboard tracking progress on implementing the A2ZERO plan —
making the plan's metrics and milestones visible to the community in
near-real-time rather than only through annual reports.

## Year 4 (a2zero-year4)

Launched in Year 4. The dashboard resolves the Year 2 commitment to
"Launch a new Ann Arbor Sustainability Dashboard" that was carried through
Year 3 without delivery and ultimately marked "missed" in the wiki. It
arrived two years late but fully — fulfilling the original intent.

No specific platform or data sources were named in the Year 4 report. The
dashboard appears to be publicly accessible via the a2zero.gov infrastructure.

## Status as of Year 4

Launched. Fulfills [[../commitments/metrics-dashboard-launch|metrics dashboard
commitment from Year 2]] (delayed 2 years; now resolved).
