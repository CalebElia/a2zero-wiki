## What it is

A millage-funded residential rebate and incentive program designed to
increase the affordability of building electrification, building efficiency,
and e-bikes for Ann Arbor residents. Administered through a 3-year contract
with Elevate (a nonprofit energy services organization).

## Year 4 (a2zero-year4)

Program designed and contract signed in Year 4; launch targeted for summer
2024. The City authorized $4M+ for residential rebates over 2.5 years.
Elevate will design and launch the program. Separately, an EV charger
discount program for multi-family and commercial property owners is also
being designed (launching summer 2024).

This program is funded by the
[[../initiatives/community-climate-action-millage|Community Climate Action
Millage]] — the first major programmatic deployment of millage funds.

## Status as of Year 4

Designed; launching summer 2024. $4M+ authorized. 3-year Elevate contract.
Fulfills [[../commitments/residential-rebates-launch|Year 3 commitment]].
