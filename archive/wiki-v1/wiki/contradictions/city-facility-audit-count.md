## Conflicting claims

**Year 1 (a2zero-year1, Strategy 3):** "completed energy audits on 6 City
facilities" — as synthesized in [[../strategies/strategy-3-building-efficiency|Strategy 3 page]].

**Year 2 (a2zero-year2, Strategy 3):** "Conducted energy audits of 4 City
facilities and developing a plan to fully decarbonize these buildings."

## The ambiguity

It is unclear whether Year 2's "4 City facilities" refers to:
(a) four additional facilities audited in Year 2 (cumulative = 10), or
(b) four facilities from the original Year 1 six now receiving
    decarbonization planning (a subset of what was already audited), or
(c) a different scope or definition of "audit" used in each year.

The Year 2 phrasing — "audits of 4 City facilities and developing a plan
to fully decarbonize these buildings" — suggests a decarbonization-planning
phase linked to these specific 4 audits, which would be consistent with
interpretation (b): these 4 are a subset of Year 1's 6 now moving to
planning stage.

## Best-guess explanation

Interpretation (b) is most plausible: Year 1 audited 6 facilities; Year 2
selected 4 of those for full decarbonization planning. But the reports do
not confirm this — the 4 facilities in Year 2 may be entirely new audits.

## Year 3 update

Year 3 states: "Began decarbonization audits with a local engineering team
at four municipal buildings, with the goal of fully decarbonizing these
buildings by 2025" (a2zero-year3, Strategy 3). This strongly supports
**interpretation (b)**: Year 2 and Year 3 are referring to the same four
buildings — Year 2 conducted energy audits; Year 3 began deeper
decarbonization audits with an engineering team. The six audits in Year 1
appear to have been a broader initial scan, from which four priority
buildings were selected for the decarbonization pathway. This does not
fully resolve the discrepancy (the Year 1 six are never listed and cross-
referenced against the Year 2/3 four), but the Year 3 language makes
interpretation (b) the most plausible reading.

## Year 4 update — new complication

Year 4 states: "Conducted decarbonization assessments at six municipal
facilities and developed plans to make these carbon neutral buildings.
Also initiated work to support decarbonization of the City's new election
center" (a2zero-year4, Strategy 3).

This introduces a new data point: Year 4 says SIX facilities, not four —
the same count as Year 1. Three possible interpretations:
(a) Year 4 added TWO new facilities beyond the Year 2/3 four, for a
    cumulative total of 6 distinct buildings assessed across Years 2–4
(b) Year 4 is referring to a different set of six buildings than Year 1
(c) Year 4 is reporting a cumulative count of all decarbonization
    assessments, and the Year 2/3 four buildings are a subset

The Year 4 report does not name the six facilities, making cross-
referencing impossible. The election center is mentioned separately as
a Year 4 initiation — suggesting it is NOT one of the six.

Interpretation (a) is most plausible: Year 3 had 4; Year 4 added 2 more.
But this remains unresolved without facility names.

## Why it matters

The total number of facilities audited determines whether this initiative
is growing (10 total) or focusing (4 out of 6). The Year 3 priority to
"create a plan and begin implementing the decarbonization of four city
buildings" suggests these 4 are the next phase, supporting interpretation (b).
Watch Year 3 for clarity on whether new audits were conducted or whether
these are the same 4.
