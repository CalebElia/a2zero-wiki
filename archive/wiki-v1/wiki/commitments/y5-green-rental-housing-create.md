Create and launch the Green Rental Housing program, providing support
resources for landlords to meet sustainability standards. This is the
fourth consecutive year this commitment has appeared in priorities without
reaching enactment:
- Year 2 priorities → not enacted
- Year 3 priorities → not reported at all
- Year 4 priorities → "significant progress"; Council consideration summer/fall 2024
- Year 5 priorities → still listed as a creation goal
