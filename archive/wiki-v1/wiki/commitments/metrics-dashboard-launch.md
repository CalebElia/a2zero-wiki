## Commitment

No metrics dashboard was mentioned in Year 1 or Year 2 progress sections.
This Year 3 commitment is to build and publish a public-facing dashboard
tracking A2ZERO goal progress. This is a meta-commitment: it creates
infrastructure for holding all other commitments accountable publicly.
Its presence as a Year 3 priority suggests the city recognizes that
narrative reporting (annual reports) is insufficient for real-time
accountability.
