## Commitment

Year 1 "Next Steps" calls for launching a whole-home "check-up" service in
Year 2. This was the precursor concept to what became the Energy Concierge —
a one-on-one service helping residents map a path to home decarbonization.

## Year 2 resolution

Partial (a2zero-year2, Strategies 2 and 3). A whole-home decarbonization
assessment tool was developed and piloted in the Bryant neighborhood. The
service was not fully launched city-wide; the Year 3 priority is to
"launch and pilot testing of energy concierge." So Year 2 delivered
the tool and a limited pilot — not the broad launch committed to.

See [[../initiatives/energy-concierge|Energy Concierge]] and
[[../commitments/energy-concierge-pilot|Year 3 Energy Concierge commitment]].
