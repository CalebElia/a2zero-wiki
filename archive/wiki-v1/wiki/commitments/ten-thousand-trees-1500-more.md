## Commitment

Cumulative trees distributed reached ~2,500 by end of Year 2 (since spring
2021). Year 3 commits to 1,500 more, which would bring the cumulative total
to ~4,000 — meaning the program would be 40% of the way to the 10,000 goal
after 3 years. At this pace, 10,000 would be reached around Year 7 (2027–28).

## Year 3 resolution

Fulfilled and exceeded. Approximately 3,250 trees were added in Year 3,
bringing the cumulative total to ~5,750 — more than double the 1,500
committed (a2zero-year3, Strategy 6). Year 4 now targets reaching the
full 10,000 milestone plus a first-ever survival study.
