## Commitment

Network grew from 92 organizations (Year 1) to over 110 (Year 2). Year 3
commits to expanding further and, notably, "unlocking greater collaboration
opportunities" — suggesting a shift from passive network membership toward
active co-programming or resource sharing. No specific count target given.

## Year 3 resolution

Fulfilled. Network grew to 120+ organizations (from 110 in Year 2). Engaged
via virtual and in-person events explicitly designed to activate collaborators
as planning and implementation partners (a2zero-year3, Strategy 7).
