## Commitment

Adds a hard deliverable to the renewable energy pathway commitment: a
strategy must go to City Council by end of Year 4. Year 3 commissioned
the Energy Options Analysis and submitted the DOE grant for the SEU
(decision mid-2024). The Council presentation is the most concrete
governance milestone in the renewable energy strategy to date.
