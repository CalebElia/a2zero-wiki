## Commitment

Year 3 commits to continued investigation of two structural pathways:
the Sustainable Energy Utility (community-owned solar + storage utility)
and traditional utility municipalization. These are the highest-stakes
options for achieving the 100% renewable goal — neither is quick or simple.
"Continued investigating" is deliberately non-committal; watch Year 3 for
whether a preferred path is named.

## Year 3 resolution

Partial. Energy Options Analysis commissioned; DOE grant submitted for the
SEU (decision mid-2024) (a2zero-year3, Strategy 1). Year 4 adds specificity:
a strategy must be brought to Council for formal consideration by year-end —
see [[../commitments/y4-renewable-energy-strategy-to-council|commitment]].
