## Commitment

4MW of solar systems were designed for City buildings in Year 2, with
installations scheduled to begin summer 2022. Year 3 commits to completing
those installations. Funded in part by a $4.5 million ARPA grant secured
in Year 2. This is a concrete, verifiable target — either the panels are
installed or they aren't.

## Year 3 resolution

Partial. ~1MW at parks facilities contracted and underway; ~3MW at Wheeler
Service Center, Water/Wastewater Treatment Plants, and municipal airport
— vendors selected, construction starting fall 2023. Full 4MW not yet
installed (a2zero-year3, Strategy 1). Also: $2.5M federal grant secured for
solar + battery storage at critical City facilities; joint City/UM RFP for
additional MW. Year 4 carries 4MW target with added storage requirement.
