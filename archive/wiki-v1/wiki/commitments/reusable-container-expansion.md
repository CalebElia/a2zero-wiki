## Commitment

Year 1 ran a reusable container pilot at four restaurants during the Green
Fare event — a single-event test. "Next Steps" commits to expanding the
pilot in Year 2.

## Year 2 resolution

Fulfilled (a2zero-year2, Strategy 5). The pilot was formalized and expanded
into the A2ZERO Reduce, Reuse, Return (A2R3) program — an ongoing program
at three named restaurants (Zingerman's Next Door Café, Ginger Deli, and
El Harissa Market Café), with additional businesses to be added continuously.
The transition from a single-event pilot to an ongoing named program with
a return-logistics system constitutes a meaningful expansion.

See [[../initiatives/a2r3|A2R3 Program]].
