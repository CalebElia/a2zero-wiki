Win a federal implementation grant (likely DOE) to fund construction of at
least a portion of the Bryant district geothermal loop. As of Year 4,
the Bryant system is in active design serving 262 households plus Bryant
Elementary, Bryant Community Center, County Mental Health, and City public
works. The $580K DOE planning grant (Year 3) funded design; implementation
requires a separate, larger grant.
