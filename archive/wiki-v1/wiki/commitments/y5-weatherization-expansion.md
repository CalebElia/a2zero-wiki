Create a weatherization expansion program specifically for income-qualified
households. Year 4 expanded weatherization services via a grant to Washtenaw
County OCED; Year 5 formalizes this into a named city program. Distinct from
(but complementary to) the Aging in Place Efficiently program.

Commitment source: a2zero-year4, Year Five Priorities, Strategy 3.
