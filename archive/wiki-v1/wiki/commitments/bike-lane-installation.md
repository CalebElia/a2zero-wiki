## Commitment

Year 1's Strategy 4 work was thin (bike discount program, bike donation,
Vision Zero support). "Next Steps" commits to installing more bike lanes
in Year 2 — a more infrastructure-facing commitment than anything reported
in Year 1.

## Year 2 resolution

Fulfilled (a2zero-year2, Strategy 4). Year 2 reports "Supported expansion
of protected bike lanes." The framing ("supported") suggests a collaborative
or enabling role rather than direct City construction — consistent with how
A2ZERO tends to operate alongside other city departments (Planning, Public
Works) rather than building infrastructure directly.
