Achieve 70% electrification of the City fleet (all vehicle types combined).
Year 4 reached 25% of light duty fleet electrified, with a 50% light duty
target by end of calendar year 2024 also stated. The 70% overall target
is ambitious given that heavy-duty vehicles (including refuse trucks) are
just beginning electrification.

Commitment source: a2zero-year4, Year Five Priorities, Strategy 2.
