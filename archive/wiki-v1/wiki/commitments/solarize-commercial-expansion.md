## Commitment

Year 1 "Next Steps" lists expansion of Solarize to commercial enterprises as
a Year 2 target. Year 1's Solarize program served only residential customers
(1.3MW across 200+ homes); commercial solar requires different financing,
permitting, and contractor relationships.

## Year 2 resolution

Partial (a2zero-year2, Strategy 1). A pilot commercial Solarize program was
designed in collaboration with the 2030 District, but not yet launched. The
Year 3 Priorities include achieving 5MW total "through Solarize (residential
and commercial)" — implying the commercial component is finally expected to
launch in Year 3. See [[../commitments/solarize-5mw-target|Year 3 5MW commitment]].
