## Commitment

Cumulative trees reached ~5,750 at end of Year 3. Reaching 10,000 in Year 4
would require ~4,250 more trees — achievable if Year 3's pace (~3,250/year)
is sustained. The survival study is new — the first explicit plan to measure
tree mortality, addressing a data gap flagged in the original initiative page.
