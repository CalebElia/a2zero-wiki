## Commitment

Co-design with community partners was underway in Year 2, with a whole-home
decarbonization assessment piloted in Bryant. Year 3 commits to the formal
launch and pilot test. This is a service-delivery commitment, not just a
design commitment — success means real residents receiving one-on-one
decarbonization roadmaps.

## Year 3 resolution

Partial. The service was designed and is actively piloting as the "Home Energy
Advisor" with partners Elevate and EcoWorks (a2zero-year3, Strategy 2) —
name changed from "Energy Concierge." Year 4 commits to the full launch. See
[[../commitments/y4-home-energy-advisor-launch|commitment]].
