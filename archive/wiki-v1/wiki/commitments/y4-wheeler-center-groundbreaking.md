## Commitment

This is the third year this commitment appears. Committed in Year 2 (target:
Year 3), carried forward to Year 4. Design has been complete since Year 2;
the groundbreaking itself has not happened. The 20MW project would be "one
of the largest landfill solar projects in the nation."

## Year 4 resolution

**Carried-forward.** No groundbreaking occurred. The city is "continuing
to strategize and adjusting plan" — suggesting a design or financing
pivot. A $5M award secured. This is the fourth year without breaking
ground (design complete since Year 2). (a2zero-year4, Strategy 1)
