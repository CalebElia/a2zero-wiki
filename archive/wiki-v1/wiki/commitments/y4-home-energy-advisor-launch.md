## Commitment

The Home Energy Advisor (formerly Energy Concierge, committed Year 1 as
"whole-home check-up") was designed and piloting in Year 3 with Elevate
and EcoWorks. Year 4 commits to the full launch — this is the third year
this general commitment has appeared (Year 1: check-up, Year 2/3: concierge,
Year 4: Home Energy Advisor). Whether the program retains the Elevate/EcoWorks
partnership structure at full launch is to be confirmed.

## Year 4 resolution

**Fulfilled.** "Launched the A2ZERO Home Energy Advisor service to help
residents determine their next best step on the path to decarbonization.
This free service is available to anyone in the City of Ann Arbor."
(a2zero-year4, Strategy 2)
