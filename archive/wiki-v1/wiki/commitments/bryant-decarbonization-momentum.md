## Commitment

Bryant was elevated in Year 2 to the goal of becoming "the first fully
decarbonized existing neighborhood in America." Year 3 commits to continuing
that momentum. Notably vague — no specific deliverable (homes completed,
dollars deployed) is named, making this one to watch for whether Year 3
provides quantitative milestones.

## Year 3 resolution

Fulfilled. $500K MI-HOPE, $180K EECBG, $580K DOE geothermal grant for
Bryant; 19 households receiving improvements; Hub 2 completed at Bryant
Community Center (a2zero-year3). Year 4 adds specific target: 20 homes
fully carbon neutral — see [[../commitments/y4-bryant-20-homes-carbon-neutral|commitment]].
