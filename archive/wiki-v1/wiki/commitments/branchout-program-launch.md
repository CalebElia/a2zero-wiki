## Commitment

Year 1 planted over 1,000 trees and secured grant funding for planting in
underserved neighborhoods. "Next Steps" names two specific Year 2 actions:
launching "BranchOut" (a named program not described elsewhere in Year 1)
and holding more FreeTree Giveaways. The BranchOut name does not appear in
Year 1's body text — it may have been a planned program name that was later
changed or folded into the existing FreeTree/Community Tree Planting
framework.

## Year 2 resolution

Partial (a2zero-year2, Strategy 6). Two FreeTree Giveaways were held in
Year 2 (fall and spring), and cumulative trees distributed reached almost
2,500. However, "BranchOut" is never mentioned by name in the Year 2
report. The FreeTree Giveaways fulfillment is clear; BranchOut's fate is
unverified — it may have been renamed, absorbed, or quietly dropped.
