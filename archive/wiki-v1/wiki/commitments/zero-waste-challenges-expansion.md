## Commitment

The Resident Race to Zero challenge had reached 829 participating households
by Year 2. Year 3 commits to continuing the challenge series and adding a
new student-focused version — likely targeting the large Ann Arbor student
population that cycles in and out of the city each year.

## Year 3 resolution

Fulfilled. A2ZERO waste challenge launched specifically for University of
Michigan students (a2zero-year3, Strategy 5). Resident challenges continued.
Ambassadors also undertook deep engagement to increase composting rates.
