Create an Ann Arbor circular economy development strategy, co-designed with
residents through the advisory commission process initiated in Year 4. The
Year 4 planning initiative set a 12–18 month window, making this achievable
in Year 5 if on schedule. This commitment has appeared since Year 2 without
producing a completed strategy. Carries forward
[[../commitments/y4-circular-economy-strategy|Year 4 commitment]].
