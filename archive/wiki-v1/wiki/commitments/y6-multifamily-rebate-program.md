**New commitment — first appearance in Year 6 priorities.** This is distinct
from the Green Rental Housing ordinance (which sets standards) and the Home
Energy Rebate program (which serves homeowners). A multi-family rebate program
would extend financial incentives specifically to rental housing occupants,
landlords, and property managers — addressing the split-incentive problem
where landlords pay for improvements that benefit tenants.

The Commercial EV Charger Program (launched Year 5) is a partial precursor —
providing free L2 chargers to multifamily property owners. This commitment
generalizes that approach to energy efficiency more broadly.
