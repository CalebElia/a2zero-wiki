## Commitment

Year 1 issued an RFP for rooftop solar at thirteen municipal facilities.
"Next Steps" commits to actual installations in Year 2 — moving from RFP
to construction.

## Year 2 resolution

Partial (a2zero-year2, Strategy 1). 4MW of solar systems were designed for
City buildings (water treatment, water recovery, multiple parks, and others),
with installations scheduled to begin summer 2022. The $4.5 million ARPA
grant secured in Year 2 funds this work. Design is complete but installations
were not yet underway at the time the Year 2 report was written.

Year 3 commits to completing the 4MW installation — see
[[../commitments/4mw-city-facility-solar|commitment]].
