Year 5 won $10M DOE grant (on hold due to federal freeze) and $3.1M MPSC
grant for Bryant electrification. Geothermal installed at Bryant CC. $1.3M
Community Action Network contract active. Year 6 continues fundraising and
neighborhood improvements.

The federal funding on hold is the primary risk — if the $10M DOE grant
is not released, the neighborhood loop cannot be built on the current timeline.
