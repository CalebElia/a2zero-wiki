## Commitment

Same 4MW target as Year 2, but now with "including multiple energy storage
systems" added — the first explicit battery storage requirement attached to
the City facility solar goal. Year 3 had contracts underway for ~4MW
(construction starting fall 2023), so this may be achievable in Year 4
if those projects complete on schedule.
