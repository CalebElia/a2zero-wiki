## Commitment

Year 2 audited 4 City facilities and began developing decarbonization plans.
Year 3 commits to completing those plans and beginning implementation. The
"four buildings" language aligns with Year 2's audit count — suggesting
these are the same four (not a new set).

See the [[../contradictions/city-facility-audit-count|audit count contradiction]]
for context on whether Year 2's "4" represents new or prior audits.

## Year 3 resolution

Partial. Decarbonization audits began with a local engineering team at
four municipal buildings; goal is full decarbonization by 2025
(a2zero-year3, Strategy 3). Audits are underway but implementation
not yet started.
