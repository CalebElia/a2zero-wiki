## Commitment

The first Bryant commitment to name a specific homes-completed target:
20 fully carbon-neutral homes. Year 3 was helping 19 households with
"health, safety, and quality of life improvements" — this Year 4 commitment
escalates to full carbon neutrality as the standard. The language "securing
funding" suggests this target is contingent on new grants.
