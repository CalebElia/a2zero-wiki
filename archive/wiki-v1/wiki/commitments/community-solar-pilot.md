## Commitment

Year 1 report lists "a community solar pilot" as planned for Year 2, under
Strategy 1 (renewable energy grid). This builds on Year 1 groundwork, where
the city reported having "helped negotiate agreements on community solar"
as part of broader VGP/carbon-offering work — this commitment appears to be
the implementation step following that negotiation.

## Status

**Fulfilled (a2zero-year2, Strategy 1).** The city intervened in utility case
U-20713 and secured a settlement that, among other terms, created "the first
community solar offering in DTE's service territory at the Wheeler Center
Solar Park." The pilot is structurally linked to the Wheeler Center landfill
solar project rather than standing alone — see
[[../initiatives/wheeler-center-solar-park|Wheeler Center Solar Park]].
No household participation count was reported in Year 2; the settlement
establishes the offering's existence, not yet its uptake.
