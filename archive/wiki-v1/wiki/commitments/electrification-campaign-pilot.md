## Commitment

Year 2 joined an RMI cohort working on electrification campaigns nationally
and launched the "Future is Electric" campaign with IBEW/NECA. The Year 3
commitment is to launch and pilot test a distinct electrification campaign.
The exact scope (citywide vs. targeted, residential vs. commercial) is not
specified.

## Year 3 resolution

Fulfilled. First annual Electrification Expo held with 25 vendors and ~450
attendees. Second annual planned for August 2023 (a2zero-year3, Strategy 2).
Also: IBEW/NECA "Future is Electric" campaign continued with informational
video series.
