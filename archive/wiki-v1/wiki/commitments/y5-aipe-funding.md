Secure funding to scale the Aging in Place Efficiently program, which
served 19 (Year 2) and 17 (Year 3) older, income-qualified residents before
closing at the end of its pilot phase. Grant funding to restart and scale it
has been sought since Year 3 without resolution.

This is the longest-running unresolved funding commitment in the wiki:
- Year 2: served 19 participants
- Year 3: served 17 more, then closed; Year 4 priority set to "fundraise"
- Year 4: AIP not named; Year 5 priority again set to "secure funding"
