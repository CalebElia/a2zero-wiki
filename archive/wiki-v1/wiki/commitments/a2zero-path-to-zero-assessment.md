## Commitment

A new named program — the "A2ZERO Assessment: Path to Zero" — to be launched
for all residents. This appears to be a scaled-up, formalized version of the
whole-home check-up concept (first committed Year 1, piloted as the Home
Energy Advisor in Year 3). The "for all residents" framing suggests a
city-wide rollout rather than a pilot. Funding may come from the newly
passed Community Climate Action Millage.

## Year 4 resolution

**Fulfilled** — as the Home Energy Advisor. The Year 4 report does not use
the "Path to Zero" name; it describes the "A2ZERO Home Energy Advisor service
to help residents determine their next best step on the path to
decarbonization...available to anyone in the City of Ann Arbor." This is the
same service, renamed. (a2zero-year4, Strategy 2)
