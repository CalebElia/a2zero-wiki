Launch the City's first microgrid (Veterans Memorial Park / Fire Station 3)
and advance the biodigester project at the Water Resource Recovery Facility.
Year 4 secured a $70K design grant for the microgrid and submitted a FEMA
BRIC construction grant. The biodigester would convert wastewater solids to
renewable energy.
