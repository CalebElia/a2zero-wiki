## Commitment

Year 2 report notes the city working with peer communities to advance a
national decarbonization coalition. The Year 3 priority extends this to
county-level collaboration, suggesting an intent to scale A2ZERO-style
approaches to other Washtenaw County municipalities.

## Year 3 resolution

Partial. OSI coordinated with Washtenaw County on their GHG inventory and
worked with regional stakeholders to explore feasibility of a regional
climate collaborative (a2zero-year3, Strategy 7). No formal collaborative
launched yet. Year 4 carries forward — see [[../commitments/y4-county-climate-action|commitment]].
