## Commitment

Hubs 2 and 3 were designed in Year 2 at Bryant Community Center and Ann
Arbor Senior Center respectively. Year 3 commits to implementation (actual
construction/buildout). Hub 1 (Northside Community Center) opened in Year 1
and demonstrated real-world function during a 5-day power outage in
August 2021.

## Year 3 resolution

Partial. Hub 2 (Bryant Community Center) completed. Hub 3 (Ann Arbor
Senior Center) not completed — Year 4 now targets hubs 3 AND 4 together
(a2zero-year3, Strategy 6). See [[../commitments/y4-resilience-hubs-3-and-4|commitment]].
