## Commitment

Year 1 began developing an LMI solar access program and won a National
Community Solar Partnership grant for technical assistance. "Next Steps"
commits to creating the program in Year 2 — the implication is that Year 1
did the groundwork and Year 2 should produce an operational program.

## Year 2 resolution

Unverified (a2zero-year2). No LMI solar program was named or described in
the Year 2 report. The utility settlement (U-20713) included reduced rates
for MIGreenPower enrollees, which has equity implications, but that is not
an LMI-specific solar access program. The NCSP technical assistance grant
is also not referenced in Year 2.

Watch Year 3 — this commitment has now carried forward twice without
resolution. See [[../initiatives/lmi-solar-access-program|LMI Solar Access Program]].
