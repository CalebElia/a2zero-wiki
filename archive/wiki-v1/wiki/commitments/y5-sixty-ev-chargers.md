Deploy 60 more EV chargers, including the next tranche of curbside public
Level 2 chargers (the first curbside chargers went live in Year 4 —
Michigan's first). The target escalated from "50 chargers" (Year 3 → Year 4)
to "60 more chargers" (Year 4 → Year 5). A grant for chargers at two dozen
more locations was submitted in Year 4.

Commitment source: a2zero-year4, Year Five Priorities, Strategy 2.
