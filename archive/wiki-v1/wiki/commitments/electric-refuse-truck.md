## Commitment

Year 1 "Next Steps" names "the commissioning of the City's first all
electric refuse truck" as a Year 2 target. This is a specific,
high-visibility, easily-verifiable commitment — a strong test case for the
wiki's contradiction/fulfillment-tracking workflow, since either the truck
exists in service by Year 2's report or it doesn't.

## Status

**Fulfilled and exceeded (a2zero-year2, Strategy 2).** Year 2 reports:
"Won awards to bring two (2) electric refuse trucks into the City's fleet" —
double the single truck committed to in Year 1. Funding came from two
separate grants: $270,000 from the American Lung Association and $406,000
from the State of Michigan (a2zero-year2, Strategy 7 grants list). Note
that "won awards" implies the trucks were funded but not necessarily yet
in active service; Year 3 ingest should confirm commissioning status.
