## Commitment

Year 2 installed 40 dual-port chargers in DDA garages and 4 DCFC at City
Hall. Year 3 targets 50 more chargers in coordination with the University
of Michigan, DDA, and State of Michigan — the multi-partner framing suggests
this will involve shared infrastructure and possibly different locations
(e.g., UM campus, utility-pole charging from the $54K pilot grant).

## Year 3 resolution

Partial. The $54K State of Michigan grant for utility-pole curbside EV
chargers is in hand; installation expected fall 2023. The 50-charger
target was not met in Year 3. Year 4 carries the same 50-charger target —
see [[../commitments/y4-fifty-ev-chargers|commitment]].
