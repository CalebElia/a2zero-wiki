## Commitment

Year 1 engaged via city-wide events (Earth Day, A2ZERO Week, Farmer's
Market, etc.) and neighborhood association meetings. "Next Steps" commits
to deepening this into more sustained neighborhood-level engagement —
implying more than one-off presentations and moving toward ongoing
relationships with specific neighborhoods.

## Year 2 resolution

Fulfilled (a2zero-year2, Strategy 7). Year 2 hosted over 100 community
events, meetings, presentations, and discussions. The Bryant neighborhood
work deepened substantially (whole-home assessment, grants, resilience hub
design). The Ambassadors program — with cohorts 2 and 3 completed — extends
reach through trained neighborhood advocates. The collaborator network grew
to 110+ organizations, many of which are neighborhood-anchored groups.
