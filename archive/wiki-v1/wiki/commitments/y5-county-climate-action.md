Collaborate with Washtenaw County and other regional stakeholders to expand
county-wide climate action. Feasibility work on a regional climate
collaborative has been underway since Year 3 without formal launch. Year 5
also includes a 3-year contract with Washtenaw 2030 District (signed Year 4)
as a formal regional partnership.
