## Commitment

Design was finalized in Year 2. Year 3 commitment is to begin physical
construction (break ground). At 20MW, this is the single largest solar
project in the A2ZERO portfolio and a milestone for both the renewable
energy grid strategy and the community solar offering established via
utility settlement U-20713.

## Year 3 resolution

Carried-forward. Year 3 continued work on the landfill solar installation
but reported no groundbreaking (a2zero-year3, Strategy 1). Year 4 carries
the same commitment — see [[../commitments/y4-wheeler-center-groundbreaking|commitment]].
