## Commitment

Two AQMesh monitors were installed in Year 2 (Kerrytown Farmers Market and
Bryant Community Center); two more were ordered and ready for Year 3
installation. Year 3 commits to at least four more — going well beyond
the two on order, suggesting additional procurement is intended. All data
is to be publicly available.

## Year 3 resolution

Partial. Two additional monitors installed in Year 3, bringing total to
four (the two ordered in Year 2 were the ones installed). Year 4 still
targets at least four more — meaning the "four more" in this commitment
was only half met. See [[../commitments/y4-four-air-quality-monitors|commitment]].
