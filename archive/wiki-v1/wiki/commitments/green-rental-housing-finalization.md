## Commitment

Draft was developed in Year 2 via a task force process. Year 3 commits to
finalization — which in context means Council consideration and adoption.
The GRH ordinance's scope (appliance electrification requirements for
rentals) makes this a significant equity and emissions lever, given Ann
Arbor's large renter population.

## Year 3 resolution

Carried-forward. Green Rental Housing is not mentioned anywhere in Year 3
report — a third consecutive year with this priority listed but no reported
progress. Year 4 re-lists "Finalizing a green rental housing initiative and
formally launching the program." See [[../commitments/y4-green-rental-housing-launch|commitment]].
