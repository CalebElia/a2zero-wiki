## Commitment

Program stood at 85 members at Year 3 with the next cohort's applications
open. Two more cohorts to push past 100. The program has grown consistently:
22 (Y1) → 50+ (Y2) → 85 (Y3) → 100+ (Y4 target). Pace has been steady;
this target is credible.

## Year 4 resolution

**Fulfilled.** 5th cohort graduated at 99 Ambassadors; 6th cohort launched,
marking the 100th Ambassador trained (a2zero-year4, Strategy 6). Milestone
delivered exactly on schedule.
