commits to training "our second Ambassadors cohort" in Year 2 — note the
report does not specify a target headcount for cohort 2, only that it will
happen.

both cohort 2 AND cohort 3, bringing total trained Ambassadors to over 50.

same year suggests accelerating program capacity. Cohort sizes are not
broken out individually — the report gives a cumulative "over 50" figure.
Year 3 target is to run two more cohorts to reach nearly 100 total
(a2zero-year2, "Year 3 Priorities").
