## Commitment

Year 4 will deploy the first millage-funded resident-facing programs:
a series of rebates and incentives to help residents electrify appliances,
improve efficiency, and install renewables. This is the direct programmatic
outcome of the Community Climate Action Millage passed in November 2023.

The design of these programs was informed by the Year 3 contractor convening,
which explicitly collected feedback "on soon-to-be created A2ZERO rebate
programs."

## Year 4 resolution

**Partial.** Program designed and $4M+ authorized; 3-year Elevate contract
signed. Local rebate program targeting launch summer 2024. Not yet live at
time of Year 4 report publication. (a2zero-year4, Strategy 2)
See [[../initiatives/home-energy-rebate-program|Home Energy Rebate Program]].
