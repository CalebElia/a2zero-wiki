## Commitment

A sustainable food purchasing policy was adopted by City Council on March
21, 2022 (Year 2). Year 3 commits to building a broader sustainable food
framework and piloting it at City facilities — moving from purchasing policy
into a more comprehensive food systems strategy.

## Year 3 resolution

Partial. A rough draft was developed with a working group of local business
owners and City staff, and implementation began (a2zero-year3, Strategy 5).
Food events (Plant-Based Challenge, Local Food Festival) also held. Year 4
carries forward "Creating a circular economy strategy co-designed with Ann Arborites"
which subsumes this work.
