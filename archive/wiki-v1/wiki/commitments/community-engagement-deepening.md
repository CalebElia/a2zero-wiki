## Commitment

One of the least specific Year 3 priorities — no metric or deliverable is
named. Year 2 hosted 100+ community events; this commitment suggests
continuing and deepening that engagement rather than a discrete new program.
Watch Year 3 for whether a metric is introduced (events count, residents
reached, etc.) or whether this remains a qualitative claim.

## Year 3 resolution

Fulfilled. 150+ community events (up from 100+ in Year 2); Community Climate
Action Millage passed; VP Kamala Harris visit; Green Fair events; school
sustainability grant program launched (a2zero-year3, Strategy 7). Engagement
deepened meaningfully in volume and institutional reach.
