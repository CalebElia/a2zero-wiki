## Commitment

Draft was advanced in Year 2 and expected to go to Council in Year 3.
Finalization implies Council adoption. Together with the commercial
benchmarking ordinance (passed Year 2), this would give Ann Arbor mandatory
energy transparency across both commercial and residential sectors.

## Year 3 resolution
