## Commitment

Year 1 completed energy audits of 6 City facilities (a2zero-year1,
Strategy 3). "Next Steps" commits to taking the next step: actual efficiency
improvements at those facilities in Year 2.

## Year 2 resolution

Partial (a2zero-year2, Strategy 3). Year 2 conducted energy audits of 4
City facilities and began developing decarbonization plans. This is planning
work, not yet implementation. Note the apparent discrepancy between 6
audits in Year 1 and 4 in Year 2 — see
[[../contradictions/city-facility-audit-count|contradiction flag]].

The Year 3 priority is to "create a plan and begin implementing the
decarbonization of four city buildings" — see
[[../commitments/four-buildings-decarbonization-plan|commitment]].
