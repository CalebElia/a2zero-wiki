Install solar at every viable city facility, or have a documented plan for
each facility not yet reached. As of Year 4 end: 250kW installed at seven
parks/recreation sites; nearly 4MW designed for eight more sites (targeted
Year 5 installation). Wheeler Center Solar Park in strategy review.

Note the deliberate hedge "or have a plan" — this commitment allows
documenting planned installations rather than requiring completed ones,
meaning it can be fulfilled by publication of facility-by-facility plans.

Commitment source: a2zero-year4, Year Five Priorities, Strategy 1.
