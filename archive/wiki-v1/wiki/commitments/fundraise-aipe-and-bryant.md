## Commitment

Both Aging in Place Efficiently (serving 19 residents in Year 2) and Bryant
decarbonization ($100K+ in grants in Year 2) are grant-dependent. Year 3
commits to continued fundraising to sustain and expand both programs.
Notable that fundraising itself — not just spending — is listed as a Year 3
priority, signaling that current funding is insufficient for desired scale.

## Year 3 resolution

Partial. Bryant: well-funded ($500K MI-HOPE, $180K EECBG, $580K DOE
geothermal — a2zero-year3). AIP: served 17 more participants, then closed
as pilot ended; ongoing funding search underway. Year 4 targets "securing
funding to scale the Aging in Place Efficiently program" as a distinct
priority.
