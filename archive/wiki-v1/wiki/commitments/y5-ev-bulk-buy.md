Launch an EV bulk buy program allowing residents to collectively purchase
electric vehicles at a discounted group rate — similar to the Solarize model
applied to vehicles. This follows the precedent of the battery backup Solarize
bulk buy (37 batteries, $101,650 in savings) piloted in Year 4.
