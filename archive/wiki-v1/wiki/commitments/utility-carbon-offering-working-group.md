## Commitment

Year 1 negotiated agreements on community solar and Voluntary Green Pricing
as part of broader utility policy work. "Next Steps" commits to creating a
working group specifically focused on carbon reduction offerings and Virtual
Power Reduction Agreements (VPAs) — a more structured, sustained engagement
with DTE on clean energy products.

## Year 2 resolution

Fulfilled (a2zero-year2, Strategy 1). The city intervened in and settled
utility case U-20713, with one of the three settlement terms being the
launch of "a working group to explore creation of carbon reduction offerings
such as a Virtual Power Reduction Agreement and 24/7 renewable power
offerings." The working group was established as part of a binding regulatory
settlement — a stronger mechanism than an informal collaborative.
