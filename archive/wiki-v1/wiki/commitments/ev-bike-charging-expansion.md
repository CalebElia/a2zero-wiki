## Commitment

Year 1 won a grant for 4 DCFCs at City Hall but reported no broader
charging infrastructure expansion. "Next Steps" commits to expanding EV and
bike charging in Year 2.

## Year 2 resolution

Fulfilled (a2zero-year2, Strategy 2). Year 2 installed 40 dual-port
chargers in Downtown Development Authority garages and 4 DCFCs at City
Hall (completing the Year 1 grant). An additional $54,000 grant from the
State of Michigan was secured for a utility-pole EV charging pilot.

Year 3 commits to 50 more chargers — see [[../commitments/fifty-ev-chargers|commitment]].
