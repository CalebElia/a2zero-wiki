## Commitment

Bryant Community Center already hosts the second resilience hub (completed
Year 3). Year 4 commits to making the building itself carbon negative —
funded by the $180K Energy Efficiency and Conservation Block Grant secured
in Year 3. The "living laboratory" framing signals intent to use it as a
showcase for replicable decarbonization approaches.
