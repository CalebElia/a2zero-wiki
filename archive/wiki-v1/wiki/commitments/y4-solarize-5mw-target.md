## Commitment

The 5MW Solarize target has now appeared in Year 2, Year 3, and Year 4
priorities. Whether it represents a cumulative total or an annual deployment
rate is ambiguous — see the [[../commitments/solarize-5mw-target|Year 2 version]]
for analysis. Year 3 reached "over 4MW" with commercial Solarize now active.

## Year 4 resolution

**Partial.** Year 4 reached 4.8MW cumulative (231 systems / 1.8MW in
calendar year 2023, the highest single-year installation). Short of 5MW.
Year 5 target set to 5.5MW. The Year 3 "over 4MW" figure is confirmed
as cumulative, not annual — resolving that earlier ambiguity.
(a2zero-year4, Strategy 1)
