## Commitment

Year 2 launched collaboration with the Planning Department to begin
integrating A2ZERO into the UDC and initiated a carbon calculator for the
plan review process. The Planning Department also removed parking minimums
from plan review requirements. Year 3 commits to full embedding — making
A2ZERO criteria part of the official comprehensive plan and UDC.

## Year 3 resolution

Partial. Comprehensive Plan RFP released with carbon neutrality and equity
as priority areas; ~400 acres rezoned to TC1 (transit corridor); transit
millage passed (effective 2024). Year 4 carries forward full UDC embedding —
see [[../commitments/y4-land-use-code-integration|commitment]].
