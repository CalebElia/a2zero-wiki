## Commitment

Year 1 regularly participated in Michigan Public Service Commission working
groups. "Next Steps" commits to continuing this engagement — monitoring
cases, intervening where needed, and testifying before legislative committees
on relevant bills and policies.

## Year 2 resolution

Fulfilled (a2zero-year2, Strategy 1). Year 2 saw active intervention in two
utility cases: U-20713 (settled, creating community solar at Wheeler Center
and the carbon reduction working group) and U-20836 (challenging DTE's
proposed rate increase and changes to solar compensation for systems with
net metering). Intervention — a formal legal process before the MPSC — is
a more intensive form of engagement than working group participation.
