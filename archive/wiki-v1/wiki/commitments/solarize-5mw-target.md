## Commitment

Year 2 ended with over 3MW cumulative from the Solarize program (residential
only). Year 3 targets 5MW — requiring ~2MW of additional deployment, with
the addition of commercial participants for the first time. The commercial
pilot was designed in Year 2 with the 2030 District but not yet launched.

## Year 3 resolution

Partial. Year 3 installed over 4MW through Solarize (residential) and launched
the commercial Solarize program (a2zero-year3, Strategy 1). The 5MW milestone
was not reached. Year 4 carries the same 5MW target — see
[[../commitments/y4-solarize-5mw-target|Year 4 commitment]].

Note: Year 3's "over 4MW" may refer to Year 3 annual deployment (not
cumulative), based on the 540 new home figure and system-size math. If so,
cumulative total through Year 3 would be 7MW+, and the Year 4 "5MW" target
would be an annual rate goal, not a cumulative one. The reporting does not
clarify this ambiguity.
