The A2Zero strategy list states the goal is to 'Reduce the miles we travel in our vehicles by at least 50%.' (source: cap-2020) However, an infographic on the same page describes the target as 'a decrease in miles traveled in vehicles by at least 40%.' (source: cap-2020) Both figures appear in the same source document. The most likely explanation is that the infographic reflects an earlier draft figure that was not updated when the strategy text was finalized, but this has not been confirmed.
