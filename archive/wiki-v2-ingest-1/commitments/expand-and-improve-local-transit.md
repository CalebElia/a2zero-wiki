This commitment aims for 95% of Ann Arbor residents to have access to transit within a 10-minute walk with frequencies of 20 minutes or faster by 2030, leading to 40% of journeys to work taken by transit. (source: cap-2020) The Get Downtown Program is planned to expand to all businesses with more than 20 employees in Ann Arbor and to Ann Arbor residents not working for a participating company by 2021. (source: cap-2020) The 10-year cost is estimated at $656,780,000, with a greenhouse gas reduction potential of 93,200 metric tons CO2 equivalent, representing 4.3% of community-wide emissions. (source: cap-2020) Assumptions include quadrupling current bus ridership and adding 2 new electric buses per year for 10 years. (source: cap-2020)
