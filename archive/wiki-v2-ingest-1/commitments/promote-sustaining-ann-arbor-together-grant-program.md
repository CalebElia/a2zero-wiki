OSI committed to administering the Sustaining Ann Arbor Together grant program, which provides small grants to residents, schools, local nonprofits, and local businesses to advance sustainability-related activities in Ann Arbor's right of way. (source: cap-2020) The program assumes $100,000 will continue to be available annually and that at least 10 grants will be awarded per year. (source: cap-2020) The estimated 10-year cost is $1,000,000, including staffing, hard, and soft costs; greenhouse gas reduction potential was not calculated. (source: cap-2020)
