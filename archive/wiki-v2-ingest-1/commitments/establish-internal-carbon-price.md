The City committed to creating an internal carbon tax whereby all City Service Areas pay a fee per ton of greenhouse gases emitted, with funds reinvested into energy efficiency improvements and renewable energy installations at City facilities. (source: cap-2020) In 2020, OSI staff were to create an internal carbon tax work plan and pilot a $5/ton charge with the finance department, increasing to $10/ton in 2021. (source: cap-2020) The goal is for all City operations to be powered with 100% clean and renewable energy by 2030. (source: cap-2020) The estimated 10-year cost is $75,000, including staffing, hard, and soft costs; greenhouse gas reduction potential was not calculated. (source: cap-2020)
