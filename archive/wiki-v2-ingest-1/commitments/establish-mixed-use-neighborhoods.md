title: Establish Mixed-Use Neighborhoods
type: commitment

This commitment focuses on updating the zoning code to allow mixed uses in residential neighborhoods so that amenities such as bakeries, grocery stores, breweries, and coffee shops are accessible in nearly all neighborhoods, increasing walkability and reducing vehicular miles driven. (source: cap-2020) The indicator of success is that by 2030, 20% of in-city trips are done by walking or biking. (source: cap-2020) The 10-year cost is estimated at $365,000, and the greenhouse gas reduction potential is captured in other actions and not independently quantified. (source: cap-2020) Zoning code amendments were planned for adoption in 2024, with a Planning Commission review of the code planned for 2028. (source: cap-2020)
