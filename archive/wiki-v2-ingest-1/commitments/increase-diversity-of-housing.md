This commitment focuses on supporting policies to increase housing opportunities within the City, with a goal of 2,000 new units of residential multi-family or ADUs built in residential neighborhoods by 2030. (source: cap-2020) Assumptions include a 7-10% increase in housing availability and 85% of new residential units located in mixed-use areas. (source: cap-2020) The 10-year cost is estimated at $250,000, with a greenhouse gas reduction potential of 2,200 metric tons CO2 equivalent, representing 0.1% of community-wide emissions. (source: cap-2020) A Comprehensive Master Plan Update was planned for completion in 2021, with zoning code amendments to be adopted by 2024 if needed. (source: cap-2020)
