A2Zero Strategy 6 commits Ann Arbor to enhancing the resilience of its people and place as one of seven overarching strategies in the Carbon Neutrality Plan. (source: cap-2020) This strategy reflects the climate adaptation component of A2Zero, designed to manage unavoidable climate impacts while ensuring investments do not disproportionately burden low-income, minority, and frontline populations. (source: cap-2020)
