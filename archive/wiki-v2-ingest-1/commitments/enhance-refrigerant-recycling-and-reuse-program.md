This action focuses on enhancing refrigerant recycling programs and discouraging refrigerant use where possible, given that refrigerants emit potent greenhouse gases. (source: cap-2020) The vision is to ban refrigerants from disposal at City of Ann Arbor landfills to drastically reduce greenhouse gas emissions from refrigerant disposal. (source: cap-2020) The indicator of success is a 95% reduction in the amount of refrigerants disposed of at City facilities. (source: cap-2020) The estimated total cost over 10 years is $179,400; greenhouse gas reduction potential has not been calculated. (source: cap-2020)
