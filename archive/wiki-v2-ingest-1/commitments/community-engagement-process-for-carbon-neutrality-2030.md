City Council directed the City Administrator to design and execute a community engagement process, beginning immediately, that culminates with a draft strategy for how the Ann Arbor community could achieve carbon neutrality around the year 2030. (source: cap-2020) Funding for public engagement was noted as available in the FY2020 Office of Sustainability and Innovations budget. (source: cap-2020)
