Ann Arbor committed to creating neighborhood and youth ambassador programs to rapidly scale up carbon neutrality work across the community. (source: cap-2020) Ambassadors would be trained and given tools to work with peers to implement actions identified in the A2Zero plan, with a goal of 100 individuals trained as youth or neighborhood ambassadors by 2025. (source: cap-2020) The Office of Sustainability and Innovations planned to partner with nonprofits such as United Way to train cohorts of low-income adult residents and youth from underserved communities, with stipends provided to those cohorts. (source: cap-2020) The estimated 10-year cost is $350,000, and greenhouse gas reduction potential was not calculated for this action. (source: cap-2020)
