title: Support Onsite Renewable Energy Generation with Battery Storage (Bulk Buys)
type: commitment
---

The City committed to establishing a bulk purchase program enabling residents and businesses to procure onsite renewable energy systems at discounted prices of 5%, 10%, or potentially 15% through economies of scale. (source: cap-2020) The goal is to achieve 78MW of local renewable energy generation on viable buildings, parcels of land, and carports by 2030, with at least one quarter of installations also including battery storage. (source: cap-2020) The initiative is projected to reduce GHG emissions by 3.7%, equivalent to 81,000 metric tons of CO2e, at an estimated 10-year cost of $605,000 including $540,000 for staffing and $65,000 for community resident stipends. (source: cap-2020) A pilot group buy in the residential sector was planned for 2020, with program scaling to continue through at least 2030. (source: cap-2020)
