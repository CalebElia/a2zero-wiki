The City of Ann Arbor committed to achieving community-wide carbon neutrality by 2030, as directed by a unanimous City Council climate emergency declaration. (source: cap-2020) Achieving this goal requires adopting new land use strategies, eliminating dependence on internal combustion engine vehicles, powering all homes, businesses, vehicles, and recreational sites with 100% renewable energy, and changing how the community interacts with materials and resources. (source: cap-2020) The City of Ann Arbor and 60+ partner organizations committed to working with community members and stakeholders to implement the A2Zero Carbon Neutrality Plan. (source: cap-2020)
