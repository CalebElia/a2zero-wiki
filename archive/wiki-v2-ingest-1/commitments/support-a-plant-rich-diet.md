This action focuses on educating the community about the benefits of reducing meat consumption and increasing consumption of fruits, vegetables, legumes, whole grains, nuts, and seeds. (source: cap-2020) The action includes exploration of a greenhouse gas emissions footprint disclosure (similar to a calorie disclosure) on food sold at City facilities to educate people on the environmental footprint of their dietary choices. (source: cap-2020) The action acknowledges that food deserts exist in the region, and aims to ensure low-income residents have access to healthy, nutritious options. (source: cap-2020) The estimated total cost over 10 years is $208,400; greenhouse gas reduction potential has not been calculated. (source: cap-2020)
