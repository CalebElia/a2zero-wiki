The City's Non-Motorized Transportation Plan aims to ensure an interconnected network of options to achieve 25% of in-city trips through non-motorized transit by 2030. (source: cap-2020) New infrastructure including sidewalks, road crossings, separated bike lanes, streetlights, and traffic devices will be needed to achieve this goal. (source: cap-2020) Assumptions include installing 5 miles of new bike lanes per year, at least 5 major mid-block crosswalks per year, and filling 90% of sidewalk gaps by 2030. (source: cap-2020) The 10-year cost is estimated at $2,400,000 including staffing, hard, and soft costs. (source: cap-2020)
