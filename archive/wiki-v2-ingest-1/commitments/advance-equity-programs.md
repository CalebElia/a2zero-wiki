The Office of Sustainability and Innovations (OSI) committed to ensuring all of its programs have an equity focus by 2028, with OSI staff reflecting the diversity of Ann Arbor and Washtenaw County. (source: cap-2020) In 2020, OSI staff were to receive and lead continuing education about equity issues and hire an equity fellow to help with aging in place efficiently work. (source: cap-2020) In 2021, OSI planned to conduct baseline equity trainings with the Energy and Environmental Commissioners, hire a summer equity fellow, and form an OSI Equity Advisory Commission. (source: cap-2020) The estimated cost over 10 years is $200,000, including staffing, hard, and soft costs; greenhouse gas reduction potential was not calculated. (source: cap-2020)
