This commitment targets a high-capacity transit and express transit system connecting Ann Arbor with Detroit, Ypsilanti, Chelsea, and Brighton by 2030, with 25% of commuter trips into and out of Ann Arbor occurring via regional transit. (source: cap-2020) Upwards of 80,000 individuals commute into Ann Arbor on any given day for work, school, or recreation, making regional transit a significant opportunity for emissions reduction. (source: cap-2020) Regional transit to Detroit was planned to be established in 2020, with service to surrounding municipalities planned for 2021. (source: cap-2020) The greenhouse gas reduction potential is captured in other actions and not independently quantified for this commitment. (source: cap-2020)
