This action involves implementation of an existing draft City plan for Construction and Demolition Waste to ensure 100% of debris is captured and repurposed, and expands the plan to require sustainable designs in new building approval processes. (source: cap-2020) The vision is to remove 80% of construction and demolition waste from the landfill and support its repurposing for other constructive uses. (source: cap-2020) Success would consist of development of an ordinance requiring construction and demolition projects to submit waste and diversion data as a component of their building permit or occupancy permit. (source: cap-2020) The estimated total cost over 10 years is $270,000; greenhouse gas reduction potential has not been calculated. (source: cap-2020)
