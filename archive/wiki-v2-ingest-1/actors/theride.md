TheRide was identified in the A2Zero planning process as a key transit provider whose bus routes could be expanded and connected to improve public transportation in Ann Arbor. (source: cap-2020) Electrifying TheRide vehicles and right-sizing transit vehicles were among the ideas considered for reducing transportation emissions. (source: cap-2020)
