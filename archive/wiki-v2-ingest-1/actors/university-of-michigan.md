The University of Michigan was one of 67 organizations that signed up as a partner of A2Zero as of early March 2020. (source: cap-2020) The University of Michigan President's Commission on Carbon Neutrality was also listed as a separate partner organization in the A2Zero planning process. (source: cap-2020)

<!-- source: cap-2020 -->
The University of Michigan is listed as a collaborator and project co-designer for Ann Arbor's Environmental Sensors Monitoring Network initiative, with the plan calling for piloting sensors throughout the city with UM and other partners in 2021. (source: cap-2020)

<!-- source: cap-2020 -->
The University of Michigan was identified in the A2Zero planning process as a potential host for community solar on university property. (source: cap-2020) The plan also considered working with the University of Michigan to create incentives for students not to bring cars to campus and to collaborate on food planning, purchasing, and waste reduction. (source: cap-2020)
