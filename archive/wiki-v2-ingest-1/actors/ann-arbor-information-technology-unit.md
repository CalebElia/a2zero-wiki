The Ann Arbor Information Technology Unit is the party responsible for implementing the Environmental Sensors Monitoring Network initiative under Strategy 6. (source: cap-2020) The unit is tasked with deploying an interconnected network of sensors and monitors throughout the city to provide real-time data on heat, air quality, waterways, and flooding.
