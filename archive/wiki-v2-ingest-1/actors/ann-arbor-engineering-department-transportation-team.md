The Ann Arbor Engineering Department Transportation Team is the party responsible for implementing the Non-Motorized Transportation Plan, with support from Ann Arbor City Council. (source: cap-2020) The team is also a co-implementer of the Tiered Parking Rates initiative and a collaborator on local transit, regional transit, and park and ride expansion initiatives.
