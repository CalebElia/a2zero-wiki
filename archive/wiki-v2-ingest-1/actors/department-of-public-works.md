The Department of Public Works is identified as the party responsible for implementation of multiple Strategy 5 actions, including expanding composting, expanding commercial recycling, requiring sustainable materials in developments, moving toward a circular economy, and enhancing the refrigerant recycling and reuse program. (source: cap-2020) The department works in collaboration with the Office of Sustainability and Innovations and external partners such as Resource Recycling Associates across these initiatives. (source: cap-2020)
