The National Wildlife Federation was one of 67 organizations that signed up as a partner of A2Zero as of early March 2020. (source: cap-2020) Partner organizations agreed to co-host a carbon neutrality event with the Office of Sustainability and Innovations, share information about the initiative with their membership and networks, and share their logo on partner messaging materials.
