The Ann Arbor Area Transit Authority (AAATA) is the party responsible for implementing both local and regional transit expansion under A2Zero Strategy 4, with support from the Regional Transit Authority of Southeast Michigan. (source: cap-2020) AAATA is also a collaborator on the Park and Ride Expansion and Tiered Parking Rates initiatives. (source: cap-2020)
