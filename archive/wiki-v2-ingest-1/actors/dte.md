DTE Energy is identified as a collaborator and project co-designer for both the Community Solar Program and the Landfill Solar Project in Ann Arbor's A2Zero CAP. (source: cap-2020) The City of Ann Arbor planned to work with DTE Energy to create a local pilot community solar program following state enabling legislation, and to develop the utility-scale landfill solar project in partnership with DTE Energy and Pittsfield Township. (source: cap-2020)

<!-- source: cap-2020 -->
DTE Energy was identified in the A2Zero planning process as a potential partner for on-bill financing for energy efficiency improvements. (source: cap-2020) The plan also considered coordinating a Workplace Charging Challenge with DTE and leveraging DTE rebates for electric vehicle charging infrastructure. (source: cap-2020) Spreading information about DTE Energy's existing energy efficiency programs was also listed as an outreach idea. (source: cap-2020)
