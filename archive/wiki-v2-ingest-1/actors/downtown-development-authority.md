The Downtown Development Authority was one of 67 organizations that signed up as a partner of A2Zero as of early March 2020. (source: cap-2020)

<!-- source: cap-2020 -->
The Downtown Development Authority (DDA) is the party responsible for implementing the Tiered Parking Rates initiative, in partnership with the Ann Arbor Engineering Department Transportation Group. (source: cap-2020) The DDA is also listed as a collaborator on the Non-Motorized Transportation Plan and Park and Ride Expansion initiatives. (source: cap-2020)
