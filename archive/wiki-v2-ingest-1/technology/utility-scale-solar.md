The City of Ann Arbor is developing a 24MW utility-scale solar project on the City-owned landfill and surrounding property in Pittsfield Township in partnership with DTE Energy. (source: cap-2020) The project includes ground-mounted solar and a pilot of solar panels installed on a retention pond, with final costs dependent on any required grid upgrades at the point of intersection. (source: cap-2020) Federal tax credits are assumed to be leveraged to reduce the cost of the project, and the landfill site must first be deemed viable through feasibility and technical assessments. (source: cap-2020)
