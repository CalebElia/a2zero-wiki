The City initiated A2Zero on November 13th, 2019 with a deadline of plan completion by mid-March 2020. (source: cap-2020) This set in motion an accelerated four-month planning period during which the Office of Sustainability and Innovations mobilized dozens of community partners and volunteers, thousands of members of the public, dozens of technical advisors, and peer communities to provide input into the Plan. (source: cap-2020)
