In November 2019, Ann Arbor City Council passed Resolution 19-2103: A Resolution in Support of Creating a plan to Achieve Ann Arbor Community-Wide Carbon Neutrality by 2030. (source: cap-2020) The resolution called on the City to work with the public to create a living plan for achieving carbon neutrality and to present that plan by Earth Day 2020. (source: cap-2020)
