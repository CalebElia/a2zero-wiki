title: Ann Arbor City-Owned Landfill
type: location
---

The City-owned landfill is the primary site for Ann Arbor's Landfill Solar Project, a utility-scale solar installation developed in partnership with DTE Energy and Pittsfield Township. (source: cap-2020) The project targets 24MW of installed solar capacity on the landfill and surrounding property, including land held in a PUD with Pittsfield Township, with full operation targeted by end of 2023. (source: cap-2020) The site also incorporates a pilot of solar panels installed on a retention pond and is planned to open for community solar subscriptions in 2026 if demand exists and enabling legislation passes. (source: cap-2020)
