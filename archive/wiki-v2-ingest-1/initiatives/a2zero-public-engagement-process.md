The A2Zero public engagement process consisted of three primary means: public events, public surveys, and regular communication via traditional and social media channels. (source: cap-2020) Staff in the Office of Sustainability and Innovations hosted 68 public events between November 13, 2019 and March 12, 2020, with a focus on engaging underrepresented constituencies and leveraging partner networks. (source: cap-2020) Three public surveys were administered: Survey 1 gathered ideas from the public, Survey 2 collected feedback on preferred actions and funding approaches, and Survey 3 identified what stakeholders needed to support implementation. (source: cap-2020) Additional outreach included sending 11,000 backpack fliers to all K-5th grade students in Ann Arbor Public Schools and developing a flyer for Ann Arbor Meals on Wheels to distribute to homebound residents. (source: cap-2020)
