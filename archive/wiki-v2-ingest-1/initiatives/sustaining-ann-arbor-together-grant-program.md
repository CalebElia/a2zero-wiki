type: initiative

The Sustaining Ann Arbor Together grant program provides small grants to residents, schools, local nonprofits, and local businesses to advance sustainability-related activities in Ann Arbor's right of way. (source: cap-2020) The program assumes $100,000 will be available annually and aims to award at least 10 grants per year. (source: cap-2020) Collaborators include nonprofit organizations serving Ann Arbor residents, neighborhood associations, the University of Michigan, and Ann Arbor Public Schools. (source: cap-2020) The program is intended to help underserved areas of the community unlock capital to address their specific sustainability concerns and opportunities. (source: cap-2020)
