The Get Downtown Program is an existing transit program that was planned to be expanded by 2021 to include all businesses with more than 20 employees in Ann Arbor, as well as Ann Arbor residents not working for a participating company. (source: cap-2020) The program expansion was identified as a key component of the strategy to expand and improve local transit under A2Zero. (source: cap-2020)
