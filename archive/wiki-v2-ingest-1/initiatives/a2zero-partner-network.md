The Office of Sustainability and Innovations sought partner organizations to support outreach, engagement, and eventual implementation of A2Zero; partners were required to co-host a carbon neutrality event, share information with their networks, and display their logo on partner messaging materials. (source: cap-2020) As of early March 2020, 67 organizations had signed up as A2Zero partners, including the National Wildlife Federation, The University of Michigan, Washtenaw County, Ann Arbor Public Schools, the Downtown Development Authority, and the NAACP Ann Arbor Chapter, among many others. (source: cap-2020) Every month from December through March, a partner messaging kit was developed and circulated to make it easy for partners to share A2Zero updates with their networks. (source: cap-2020)
