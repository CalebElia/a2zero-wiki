The Expand Commercial Recycling initiative uses incentives, education, and enforcement policies to achieve at least 30% commercial sector recycling rates in Ann Arbor. (source: cap-2020) The Department of Public Works, with support from Resource Recycling Associates, is responsible for implementation. (source: cap-2020) The SWRMP estimates a range of 1,700 to 4,400 tons of waste diverted from landfill as a result of increased commercial sector recycling participation. (source: cap-2020) The 10-year cost estimate is $18,400,000, with a projected greenhouse gas reduction of 5,600 metric tons CO2e (0.3% of community-wide emissions). (source: cap-2020)
