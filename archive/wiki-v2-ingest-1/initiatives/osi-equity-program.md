OSI's Equity Program works to ensure that equity is embedded in all OSI programs, stakeholder engagement, and advisory relationships, with a vision that by 2028 all OSI programs have an equity focus and staff reflect the diversity of Ann Arbor and Washtenaw County. (source: cap-2020) Collaborators include Washtenaw County's Racial Equity Program Manager, Dr. Tony Reames, the Urban Sustainability Directors Network, the City Administrator, all City Service Areas, OSI's Equity Fellow, and the City of Ann Arbor's Diversity, Equity and Inclusion Manager. (source: cap-2020) The program targets Ann Arbor City employees, staff of allied organizations, Ann Arbor residents of color, and low-income Ann Arbor residents and workers. (source: cap-2020)
