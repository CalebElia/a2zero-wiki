title: Expand and Improve Local Transit
type: initiative
---

This initiative aims to expand and improve local transit services so that 95% of Ann Arbor residents have access to transit within a 10-minute walk with frequencies of 20 minutes or faster by 2030. (source: cap-2020) The initiative includes building bus rapid transit along transportation corridors and expanding the Get Downtown Program to all businesses with more than 20 employees and to Ann Arbor residents not working for a participating company. (source: cap-2020) The 10-year cost is estimated at $656,780,000, with a greenhouse gas reduction potential of 93,200 metric tons CO2 equivalent, representing 4.3% of community-wide emissions. (source: cap-2020) Assumptions include quadrupling current bus ridership and adding 2 new electric buses per year for 10 years. (source: cap-2020)
