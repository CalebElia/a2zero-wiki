title: Local Tree Canopy Preservation and Enhancement
type: initiative
---

The Local Tree Canopy Preservation and Enhancement initiative involves conducting an analysis and shared inventory of the local tree canopy and updating the master plan to incentivize shade tree and vegetation planting for private projects. (source: cap-2020) The City Forester/Public Works is the party responsible for implementation, with support from the Office of Sustainability and Innovations. (source: cap-2020) The plan assumes the city will continue planting 1,000 trees per year on city property and in rights-of-way, and that the community will plant 1,000 trees per year on private property, with a single tree estimated to absorb 48 pounds of CO2 over the plan's duration. (source: cap-2020) The initiative is projected to reduce community-wide emissions by 0.02% (approximately 450 MTCO²e) at an estimated 10-year cost of $1,160,000. (source: cap-2020)
