title: Tiered Parking Rates
type: initiative

The Tiered Parking Rates initiative establishes a sliding parking fee structure with lower rates for low-income residents, residents with disabilities, and EV drivers, deploying smart metering and dynamic pricing. (source: cap-2020) Excess on-street parking in the DDA will be removed and replaced with infrastructure for pedestrians, bicyclists, transit, and street activities, and all parking minimums will be eliminated with low parking maximums set through the plan review process. (source: cap-2020) A pilot of dynamic parking rates in the DDA was planned for 2021, with a full-scale launch of tiered rates planned for 2022. (source: cap-2020) The 10-year cost is estimated at $4,500,000. (source: cap-2020)
