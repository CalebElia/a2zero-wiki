The A2Zero public engagement campaign launched on November 13, 2019 at Cobblestone Farm with over 250 individuals attending the public launch event. (source: cap-2020) Between November 2019 and March 2020, the city conducted at least 69 documented public engagement events spanning neighborhood associations, high schools, city departments, civic organizations, and business groups. (source: cap-2020) Events included town halls, tabling at farmers markets and community centers, presentations to city commissions, and outreach to environmental justice communities. (source: cap-2020) At least 11 planned events in March 2020 were cancelled or postponed due to the Coronavirus Pandemic. (source: cap-2020)
