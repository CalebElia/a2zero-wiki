The Refrigerant Recycling and Reuse Program focuses on enhancing refrigerant recycling and discouraging refrigerant use where possible, given that refrigerants emit potent greenhouse gases. (source: cap-2020) The program focuses on education and explores penalties for violations, with a goal of a 95% reduction in the amount of refrigerants disposed of at City facilities. (source: cap-2020) The Department of Public Works, with support from the Office of Sustainability and Innovations, is responsible for implementation. (source: cap-2020) The 10-year cost estimate is $179,400; greenhouse gas reduction potential has not been calculated. (source: cap-2020)
