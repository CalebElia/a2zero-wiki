type: initiative

The Park and Ride Expansion initiative proposes at least a 10x increase in park and ride spots from the current 1,300 available free spaces, with new lots planned to begin construction in 2023. (source: cap-2020) The initiative integrates EV charging and seamless connection to public transit to intercept commuters at the city border. (source: cap-2020) The 10-year cost is estimated at $170,000,000, excluding land purchase costs, with a greenhouse gas reduction potential of 28,400 metric tons CO2 equivalent, representing 1.3% of community-wide emissions. (source: cap-2020)
