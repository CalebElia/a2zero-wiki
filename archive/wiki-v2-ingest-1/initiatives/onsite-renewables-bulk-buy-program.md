title: Onsite Renewable Energy Generation with Battery Storage (Bulk Buys)
type: initiative
---

The Onsite Renewables Bulk Buy program enables residents and businesses to procure solar, geothermal, and energy storage systems at discounted prices by achieving economies of scale through group purchasing. (source: cap-2020) The program targets a goal of 78MW of local renewable energy generation by 2030, with at least one quarter of installations also incorporating battery storage to enhance community resilience. (source: cap-2020) The Office of Sustainability and Innovations' Solarize Coordinator leads implementation, collaborating with City Building, Rental, and Inspection Services, renewable energy contractors, Michigan Saves, and potentially local banks. (source: cap-2020) A pilot group buy in the residential sector was planned for 2020, with program revisions and scaling to follow based on lessons learned. (source: cap-2020)
