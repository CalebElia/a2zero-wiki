The first A2Zero Town Hall was a public engagement event held as part of the A2Zero planning process to gather community input on climate action ideas. (source: cap-2020) Ideas generated at the Town Hall, along with input from public surveys and four technical advisory committees, informed the development of the A2Zero Living Carbon Neutrality Plan. (source: cap-2020)
