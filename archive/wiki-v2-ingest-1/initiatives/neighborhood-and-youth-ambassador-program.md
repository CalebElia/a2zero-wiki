The Neighborhood and Youth Ambassador Program seeks to train residents to work with their peers to implement A2Zero actions related to energy, water, waste, transportation, and local food. (source: cap-2020) The Office of Sustainability and Innovations planned to partner with nonprofits like United Way to train cohorts of low-income adults and youth from underserved communities as Climate Ambassadors, with stipends provided to those cohorts. (source: cap-2020) The program targets training 100 individuals as youth or neighborhood ambassadors by 2025. (source: cap-2020) Collaborators include Ann Arbor Public Schools, Community Action Network, Neutral Zone, and A2Zero youth representatives. (source: cap-2020)
