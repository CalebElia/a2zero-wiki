The Office of Sustainability and Innovations created a meeting-in-a-box kit that included materials so individuals could host their own A2Zero events. (source: cap-2020) The toolkit included a hosting guide, a slide deck with talking points and discussion questions, a terminology sheet, an A2Zero overview sheet, a climate change overview document, a sign-in sheet, links to relevant surveys, an event results form, trivia questions, and a photo release form. (source: cap-2020)
