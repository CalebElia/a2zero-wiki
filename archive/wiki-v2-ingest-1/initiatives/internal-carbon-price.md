The Internal Carbon Price initiative creates a financial mechanism whereby all City Service Areas pay a fee per ton of greenhouse gases emitted, with funds reinvested into energy efficiency improvements and renewable energy installations at City facilities. (source: cap-2020) OSI planned to pilot a $5/ton charge in 2020, increase fleet rates to $10/ton in 2021, and review the program in 2022. (source: cap-2020) Collaborators include Finance, Information Technology, DTE Energy, the City Administrator, Consumers Energy, and Fleet and Facilities. (source: cap-2020)
