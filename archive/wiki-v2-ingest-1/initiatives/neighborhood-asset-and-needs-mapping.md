type: initiative

The Neighborhood Asset and Needs Mapping initiative involves working with neighborhoods to systematically identify local assets and needs, then leveraging those assets to create plans that address community needs. (source: cap-2020) The Office of Sustainability and Innovations is responsible for implementation, with support from Emergency Management and collaborators including the Red Cross, Meals on Wheels, Neighborhood Associations, and Ann Arbor Public Schools. (source: cap-2020) The goal is for every neighborhood in Ann Arbor to have an asset map and know how to activate it in the case of an emergency by 2025. (source: cap-2020) The estimated 10-year cost is $300,000. (source: cap-2020)
