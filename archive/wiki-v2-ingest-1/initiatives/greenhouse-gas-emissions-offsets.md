title: Greenhouse Gas Emissions Offsets
type: initiative
---

The City planned to purchase and retire renewable energy credits and carbon offsets to cover greenhouse gas emissions that cannot be locally reduced, with offsets used only to close the gap between local actions and the 2030 carbon neutrality goal. (source: cap-2020) The first offset purchase was planned for 2028, with the program expected to achieve 13.6% of community-wide emissions reductions (298,500 MTCO2e) if the University of Michigan participates in all programs. (source: cap-2020) The City aimed to invest offset funds in programs that decrease income and racial justice inequities, including potentially in the global south. (source: cap-2020) Collaborators include the Energy Commission, Environmental Commission, and University of Michigan. (source: cap-2020)
