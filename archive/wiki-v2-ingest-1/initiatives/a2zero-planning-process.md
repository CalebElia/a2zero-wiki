The A2Zero planning process spanned 82 working days, from November 13th, 2019 to mid-March 2020, and was designed as an accelerated four-month plan creation period. (source: cap-2020) The process involved three phases — Ideation, Initial Prioritization, and Quantify and Determine 'How' — drawing on inputs from peer cities, public input, staff expertise, technical advisor expertise, and published literature. (source: cap-2020) Public engagement included 68 public events, 3 public surveys, and support from 67 partner organizations as of early March 2020. (source: cap-2020) Elevate Energy was engaged to assess the greenhouse gas reduction potential and costs associated with each action included in the final Plan. (source: cap-2020)
