Four Technical Advisory Committees (TACs) were established as part of the A2Zero planning process, covering energy, mobility, resource reduction, and adaptation and resilience. (source: cap-2020) Over 80 individuals volunteered their time as technical advisors on at least one of the four committees. (source: cap-2020) Technical advisors helped identify potential actions, evaluate actions including those recommended by the public, and create a prioritized list of candidate strategies for potential inclusion in the final Plan. (source: cap-2020)

Four technical advisory committees (TACs) were established to ensure community experts could help identify what would be technically needed to achieve carbon neutrality: energy, mobility, resource reduction, and adaptation and resilience. (source: cap-2020) Over 80 individuals volunteered their time as technical advisors on at least one of the four committees. (source: cap-2020) Technical advisors helped identify potential actions, evaluate actions including those recommended by the public, and create a prioritized list of candidate strategies for potential inclusion in the final plan. (source: cap-2020)

Four technical advisory committees were convened as part of the A2Zero planning process to provide expert input on climate action ideas. (source: cap-2020) Their recommendations, along with public survey responses and town hall input, contributed to the full list of ideas considered for the A2Zero plan. (source: cap-2020)
